# 笔记13 挤压油膜阻尼器：工作原理、模型与技术问题
# NOTES 13. Squeeze Film Dampers: Operation, Models and Technical Issues

> © Dr. Luis San Andrés (2010) — Texas A&M University
> 转子动力学讲义系列 第13篇 / Rotordynamics Lecture Notes, No. 13

---

**English:** Squeeze film bearing dampers are lubricated elements providing viscous damping in mechanical systems. Squeeze film dampers in rotating machinery provide structural isolation, reduce the amplitudes of rotor response to imbalance, and in some instances, assist to suppress rotordynamic instability.

**中文：** 挤压油膜轴承阻尼器（squeeze film bearing damper）是一种润滑元件，可在机械系统中提供黏性阻尼（viscous damping）。旋转机械中的挤压油膜阻尼器（squeeze film damper, SFD）能够提供结构隔振，降低转子对不平衡量（imbalance）响应的振幅，并在某些情况下有助于抑制转子动力学失稳（rotordynamic instability）。

---

## 背景
## Background

**English:** The most commonly recurring problems in rotordynamics are excessive steady state synchronous vibration levels and subsynchronous rotor instabilities. The first problem may be reduced by improved balancing, or by introducing modifications into the rotor-bearing system to move the system critical speeds out of the operating range, or by introducing external damping to limit peak amplitudes at traversed critical speeds. Subsynchronous rotor instabilities may be avoided by eliminating the instability mechanism, by rising the natural frequency of the rotor-bearing system as high as possible, or by introducing damping to increase the onset rotor speed of instability [Vance 1988, Childs 1993].

**中文：** 转子动力学中最常反复出现的问题是过大的稳态同步振动（synchronous vibration）水平和亚同步转子失稳（subsynchronous rotor instability）。第一类问题可以通过改善动平衡来减小，或通过对转子-轴承系统进行改造以使系统临界转速（critical speed）移出工作转速范围，或通过引入外部阻尼以限制穿越临界转速时的峰值振幅来减小。亚同步转子失稳则可以通过消除失稳机制、尽可能提高转子-轴承系统的固有频率（natural frequency），或引入阻尼以提高失稳起始转速（onset speed of instability）来避免 [Vance 1988, Childs 1993]。

---

**English:** Lightweight, high performance engines exhibit a trend towards increased flexibility leading to a high sensitivity to imbalance with large vibration levels and reduced reliability. Squeeze film dampers (SFDs) are essential components of high-speed turbomachinery since they offer the unique advantages of dissipation of vibration energy and isolation of structural components, as well as the capability to improve the dynamic stability characteristics of inherently unstable rotor-bearing systems. SFDs are used primarily in aircraft jet engines to provide viscous damping to rolling element bearings which themselves have little or no damping. One other important application is related to high performance compressor units where SFDs are installed in series with tilting pad bearings to reduce (soften) bearing support stiffness while providing additional damping as a safety mechanism to prevent rotordynamic instabilities. In addition, in geared compressors, the SFD assists to reduce and isolate multiple frequency excitations transmitted through the bull gear, for example. [San Andrés, 2002].

**中文：** 轻质、高性能发动机呈现出柔性增大的趋势，从而对不平衡量高度敏感，伴随大振动水平和可靠性下降。挤压油膜阻尼器（SFD）是高速旋转机械的关键部件，因为它们具有独特的优势：可耗散振动能量、隔离结构部件，并能改善本质上不稳定的转子-轴承系统的动态稳定性特征。SFD 主要用于航空喷气发动机，为本身几乎没有阻尼的滚动轴承（rolling element bearing）提供黏性阻尼。另一项重要应用与高性能压缩机机组有关，其中 SFD 与可倾瓦轴承（tilting pad bearing）串联安装，以降低（软化）轴承支撑刚度，同时提供附加阻尼，作为防止转子动力学失稳的安全机制。此外，在齿轮传动压缩机中，SFD 有助于减小并隔离例如经由大齿轮（bull gear）传递的多频激励。[San Andrés, 2002]。

---

**English:** Zeidan et al. [1996] give a history of the SFD in jet engines and detail design practices for successful SFD operation in commercial turbomachinery. Adilleta and Della Pietra [2002] provide a comprehensive review of the relevant analytical and experimental work conducted on SFDs. San Andrés and Delgado [2007] discuss more recent SFD experimental research and present a mechanically sealed SFD impervious to air entrainment.

**中文：** Zeidan 等人 [1996] 给出了 SFD 在喷气发动机中的发展历史，并详述了商用旋转机械中 SFD 成功运行的设计实践。Adilleta 与 Della Pietra [2002] 对 SFD 相关的分析与实验工作进行了全面综述。San Andrés 与 Delgado [2007] 讨论了较新的 SFD 实验研究，并介绍了一种采用机械密封、不受空气吸入（air entrainment）影响的 SFD。

---

**English:** In spite of the many successful applications, industry often recognizes that the design of SFDs is based on overly simplified predictive models that either fail to incorporate or simply neglect unique features (structural and fluidic) that affect the damper dynamic force performance. Actual damper performance can range from erratic to non-functioning depending on the operating conditions. Issues such as lubricant cavitation or air entrainment are of fundamental interest [San Andrés and Diaz, 2003].

**中文：** 尽管有诸多成功应用，工业界常常认识到，SFD 的设计建立在过于简化的预测模型之上，这些模型要么未能纳入、要么干脆忽略了影响阻尼器动态力性能的独特特征（结构方面与流体方面）。实际阻尼器的性能可能从无规律变化到完全失效，取决于工况。诸如润滑油空化（lubricant cavitation）或空气吸入等问题具有根本性的研究价值 [San Andrés and Diaz, 2003]。

## 应用基础
## Application fundamentals

**English:** Figure 1 shows a typical SFD configuration consisting of an inner nonrotating journal and a stationary outer bearing, both nearly equal in diameter. The journal is mounted on the external race of a rolling element bearing and prevented from spinning with loose pins or a squirrel cage that provides a centering elastic mechanism. The annular squeeze film, typically less than 0.250 mm, between the journal and housing is filled with a lubricant provided as a splash from the rolling element bearing lubrication system or by a dedicated pressurized delivery. In operation, as the journal moves due to dynamic forces acting on the system, the fluid is displaced to accommodate these motions. As a result, hydrodynamic squeeze film pressures exert reaction forces on the journal and provide for a mechanism to attenuate transmitted forces and to reduce the rotor amplitude of motion.

**中文：** 图 1 展示了一个典型的 SFD 结构，由内部的非旋转轴颈（journal）和静止的外部轴承组成，二者直径几乎相等。轴颈安装在滚动轴承的外圈上，并通过松配合销钉或提供对中弹性机构的鼠笼（squirrel cage）来防止其自转。轴颈与壳体之间的环形挤压油膜（典型厚度小于 0.250 mm）充满润滑油，润滑油由滚动轴承润滑系统飞溅供油，或由专用增压供油系统提供。运行时，当轴颈在作用于系统的动态力作用下产生运动时，流体被排挤以适应这些运动。其结果是，流体动压的挤压油膜压力对轴颈施加反作用力，从而提供一种衰减传递力、降低转子运动振幅的机制。

**English:** Figure 2 shows conceptual views of intershaft dampers for multiple-spool gas turbine engines. These dampers are subject to whirl motions resulting from the combined imbalance response of both low speed (LS) and high speed (HS) rotors. Most SFDs in US aircraft engines incorporate the arrangements in Figure 2(a & b) where the journal (and rolling element bearing) is elastically supported, and the bearing is rigidly attached to the engine frame. The (soft) spring support and squeeze film damper "see" the same deflections though the dynamic loads divide unequally between them.

**中文：** 图 2 展示了多转子燃气涡轮发动机中转子间阻尼器（intershaft damper）的概念视图。这类阻尼器承受由低速（LS）转子与高速（HS）转子组合不平衡响应所引起的涡动（whirl）运动。美国航空发动机中大多数 SFD 采用图 2(a 与 b) 所示的布置方式，其中轴颈（及滚动轴承）被弹性支撑，而轴承刚性连接到发动机机架上。（软）弹簧支撑与挤压油膜阻尼器"感受"到相同的挠度，尽管动态载荷在二者之间不均等地分配。

**English:** Dampers in jet engines operate with low values of external pressurization (2 or 3 bar max.) to avoid excessive weight and volume in the lubrication system. Note also that most aircraft engines do not use any type of hydrodynamic journal bearings to avoid the risk of fluid film bearing induced instabilities. (However, in some dual shaft jet engines, the inter-spool fluid film bearing, shown in Figure 2a, is known to be a source of such instabilities).

**中文：** 喷气发动机中的阻尼器以较低的外部增压值（最高 2 或 3 bar）运行，以避免润滑系统过重和体积过大。还需注意，大多数航空发动机不使用任何类型的流体动压滑动轴承（hydrodynamic journal bearing），以避免油膜轴承诱发失稳的风险。（然而，在某些双轴喷气发动机中，图 2a 所示的转子间油膜轴承已知是此类失稳的来源之一。）

---

> [!important] 关键设计考量 / Critical design consideration
>
> **English:** The amount of damping produced is the critical design consideration. If damping is too large, the SFD acts as a rigid constraint to the rotor-bearing system with large forces transmitted to the supporting structure. If damping is too light, the damper is ineffective and likely to permit large amplitudes of vibratory motion with likely subsynchronous motions. Note also that a damping element to be effective needs to be "soft", thus allowing for motion at the location of the support, in particular for the modes of vibration of interest.
>
> **中文：** 所产生的阻尼量是关键的设计考量。若阻尼过大，SFD 将对转子-轴承系统起到刚性约束的作用，从而向支撑结构传递大的力。若阻尼过小，阻尼器将无效，并可能允许大振幅的振动运动，且很可能出现亚同步运动。还需注意，一个有效的阻尼元件需要是"软"的，从而允许支撑位置发生运动，尤其是对于所关注的振动模态。

---

**English:** The damper geometry (length, diameter and clearance), operating speed and fluid properties (density and viscosity) determine, on first instance, the dynamic forced performance of SFDs. However, there are other important considerations that ultimately determine an appropriate operation.

**中文：** 阻尼器几何参数（长度、直径和间隙）、工作转速以及流体性质（密度和黏度）首先决定了 SFD 的动态受迫性能。然而，还有其他重要的考量因素，最终决定了恰当的工作状态。

---

**English:** The relevant issues are:
a) kinematics of journal (tied to rotor system and acting forces)
b) level of supply pressure for adequate flow rate and cooling,
c) feeding and end sealing mechanisms,
d) fluid inertia effects,
e) type of lubricant dynamic cavitation (vapor or gaseous) or air ingestion and entrapment.

**中文：** 相关问题包括：
a) 轴颈的运动学（与转子系统及作用力相关联）；
b) 为获得足够流量和冷却所需的供油压力水平；
c) 供油与端部密封机制；
d) 流体惯性（fluid inertia）效应；
e) 润滑油动态空化类型（蒸气空化或气体空化），或空气吸入与滞留（air ingestion and entrapment）。

## SFD 动态受迫性能模型
## Models for SFD dynamic forced performance

**English:** Most dampers in practice are of short axial length, L/D < 0.50, and accommodate some type of end seals to increase their damping capability. SFDs include additional features such as high resistance orifices for pressure delivery and discharge and/or deep grooves acting as flow sources or sinks of uniform pressure.

**中文：** 实际中大多数阻尼器轴向长度较短，L/D < 0.50，并配有某种端部密封以提高阻尼能力。SFD 还包括其他特征，例如用于供压和排油的高阻节流孔，和/或作为均压流体源或汇的深槽。

---

**English:** Squeeze film damper reaction forces and force coefficients are conveniently divided into two major types related to the specific journal center kinematics. For imbalance response analyses, SFD forces are obtained under the assumption of circular centered orbits. The model is applicable when the rotor traverses a critical speed, for example, where the imbalance force induces large amplitude orbital motions as the system may have little damping. On the other hand, for rotordynamic critical speed and stability analyses, SFD force coefficients are obtained for small amplitude journal center motions about a static (equilibrium) position. Only recently, computational tools analyze rotor-bearing system transient response events by considering the instantaneous SFD reaction forces as a function of the time varying journal kinematics that satisfy the equations of motion of the rotating system.

**中文：** 挤压油膜阻尼器的反作用力与力系数（force coefficient）可方便地依据具体的轴颈中心运动学分为两大类。对于不平衡响应分析，SFD 力是在圆形对中轨道（circular centered orbit）假设下求得的。该模型适用于例如转子穿越临界转速的情况，此时不平衡力会诱发大振幅的轨道运动，因为系统可能阻尼很小。另一方面，对于转子动力学临界转速和稳定性分析，SFD 力系数是针对轴颈中心绕某静态（平衡）位置的小振幅运动求得的。直到最近，计算工具才能够通过将瞬时 SFD 反作用力视为满足旋转系统运动方程的时变轴颈运动学的函数，来分析转子-轴承系统的瞬态响应事件。

---

**English:** Figure 3 depicts a schematic view of a journal whirling within its bearing of radius R ( ½ diameter D) and length L. Lubricant of density ρ and viscosity μ fills the radial clearance c between the bearing and its journal. The film thickness h is squeezed as the journal whirls and displaces fluid. The film thickness equals

**中文：** 图 3 描绘了一个在其轴承内涡动的轴颈示意图，轴承半径为 R（直径 D 的一半），长度为 L。密度为 ρ、黏度为 μ 的润滑油充满轴承与轴颈之间的径向间隙 c。当轴颈涡动并排挤流体时，油膜厚度 h 被挤压。油膜厚度等于

$$h = c + \left(e_{X_o} + \Delta e_X(t)\right)\cos(\Theta) + \left(e_{Y_o} + \Delta e_Y(t)\right)\sin(\Theta) \tag{1}$$

---

**English:** where $\left(e_{X_o}, e_{Y_o}\right)$ and $\left(\Delta e_X(t), \Delta e_Y(t)\right)$ denote the static and dynamic components of journal motion, respectively. In operation, the dynamic journal motions coincide with the rotor speed for synchronous excitation from rotor imbalance, for example.

**中文：** 其中 $\left(e_{X_o}, e_{Y_o}\right)$ 和 $\left(\Delta e_X(t), \Delta e_Y(t)\right)$ 分别表示轴颈运动的静态分量和动态分量。运行时，例如对于由转子不平衡引起的同步激励，动态轴颈运动与转子转速一致。

---

*Figure 3 / 图 3：涡动轴颈视图及分析所用坐标系。图中标注 $\Delta e$ = 轴颈动态位移；$\omega$ = 涡动频率；$h$ = 油膜厚度；$R$ = 半径；$x = \Theta R$。*

*Fig. 3. View of whirling journal and coordinate system for analysis.*

---

**English:** Unlike in conventional oil lubricated journal bearings, fluid inertia affects the performance of SFDs due to their typically larger clearances and operation at high frequencies. The squeeze film Reynolds number $Re_s = \frac{\rho \omega c^2}{\mu}$ ranges from one to 50 in most practical applications. Fluid inertia effects are relevant in dampers with large clearances, using light viscous lubricants and operating at high frequencies (well above 10,000 rpm). In general, large clearance SFDs generate significant direct added mass coefficients that may lower significantly the critical speeds of compact rotating machinery, as in some small jet engine applications.

**中文：** 与传统的油润滑滑动轴承不同，由于 SFD 通常间隙较大且在高频下运行，流体惯性会影响其性能。挤压油膜雷诺数（squeeze film Reynolds number）$Re_s = \frac{\rho \omega c^2}{\mu}$ 在大多数实际应用中取值在 1 到 50 之间。流体惯性效应在间隙大、使用低黏度润滑油并在高频（远高于 10,000 rpm）下运行的阻尼器中尤为显著。一般而言，大间隙 SFD 会产生显著的直接附加质量系数（direct added mass coefficient），这可能会大幅降低紧凑型旋转机械的临界转速，例如某些小型喷气发动机的应用中。

---

**English:** Considering temporal fluid inertia effects only, the equation governing the generation of the dynamic pressure field P is

**中文：** 仅考虑时间性流体惯性（temporal fluid inertia）效应时，控制动态压力场 P 生成的方程为

$$\frac{\partial}{R \partial \Theta}\left(h^3 \frac{\partial P}{R \partial \Theta}\right) + \frac{\partial}{\partial z}\left(h^3 \frac{\partial P}{\partial z}\right) = 12\mu\frac{\partial h}{\partial t} + \left(\rho h^2\right)\frac{\partial^2 h}{\partial t^2} \tag{2}$$

---

**English:** Numerical solutions to Eqn. (2) with specific boundary conditions and accounting for lubricant cavitation (vapor or gas) are readily available [San Andrés, 2002]. Detailed physics based solutions modeling air ingestion and entrapment are yet to appear. The phenomenon is exceedingly complex, only understood in a time ensemble averaged manner, as described by San Andrés and Diaz [2003].

**中文：** 在特定边界条件下并计入润滑油空化（蒸气或气体）的方程 (2) 的数值解已可方便获得 [San Andrés, 2002]。然而，对空气吸入与滞留进行建模的、基于详细物理机理的解尚未出现。该现象极为复杂，目前仅能以时间系综平均（time ensemble averaged）的方式加以理解，如 San Andrés 与 Diaz [2003] 所述。

## SFD 转子动力学力系数
## SFD rotordynamic force coefficients

**English:** SFD reaction forces due to small amplitude journal center motions about a static eccentric or off-centered position $\left(e_{X_o} = e_s, e_{Y_o} = 0\right)$, as shown in Fig. 4, are of importance in the evaluation of critical speeds and stability of rotor-bearing systems mounted on dampers with soft or no centering springs. The damper forces are represented in the linearized form

**中文：** 如图 4 所示，由轴颈中心绕某静态偏心或偏置位置 $\left(e_{X_o} = e_s, e_{Y_o} = 0\right)$ 的小振幅运动所引起的 SFD 反作用力，对于评估安装在采用软对中弹簧或无对中弹簧的阻尼器上的转子-轴承系统的临界转速和稳定性具有重要意义。阻尼器力以线性化形式表示为

$$\begin{bmatrix} F_X \\ F_Y \end{bmatrix} = -\begin{bmatrix} C_{XX} & C_{XY} \\ C_{YX} & C_{YY} \end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix} V_X \\ V_Y \end{bmatrix} - \begin{bmatrix} M_{XX} & M_{XY} \\ M_{YX} & M_{YY} \end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix} A_X \\ A_Y \end{bmatrix} \tag{3}$$

---

**English:** where $(V_X, V_Y)$ and $(A_X, A_Y)$ are the instantaneous journal center velocities and accelerations in the X and Y directions, respectively. $\left(C_{\alpha\beta}, M_{\alpha\beta}\right)_{\alpha,\beta=X,Y}$ are the damping and inertia force coefficients, respectively. Recall that a SFD does not produce direct stiffnesses, i.e. without journal spinning, a damper cannot generate film pressures given a journal static displacement.

**中文：** 其中 $(V_X, V_Y)$ 和 $(A_X, A_Y)$ 分别是轴颈中心在 X 和 Y 方向上的瞬时速度和加速度。$\left(C_{\alpha\beta}, M_{\alpha\beta}\right)_{\alpha,\beta=X,Y}$ 分别是阻尼力系数和惯性力系数。需要记住，SFD 不产生直接刚度（direct stiffness），即在轴颈不自转的情况下，给定一个轴颈静态位移，阻尼器无法产生油膜压力。

**English:** Some dampers are designed with feed and discharge grooves to ensure a continuous flow of lubricant through the squeeze film lands, see Figure 8. ## 挤压油膜阻尼器中的润滑油空化与空气吸入
## Lubricant cavitation vs. air ingestion in squeeze film dampers

**English:** Zeidan et al. [1996] identify SFD operation with distinct types of dynamic fluid cavitation (vapor or gas) and a regime due to air ingestion and entrapment. The appearance of a particular condition depends on the damper type (sealed or open to ambient), magnitude of supply pressure and flow rate, whirl frequency, and magnitude of dynamic load producing (small or large) journal excursions within the film clearance.

**中文：** Zeidan 等人 [1996] 辨识了 SFD 运行中不同类型的动态流体空化（蒸气或气体）以及由空气吸入和滞留所导致的工况。某一特定状态的出现取决于阻尼器类型（密封式或通向环境的开放式）、供油压力和流量的大小、涡动频率，以及产生轴颈在油膜间隙内（小或大）偏移的动态载荷的大小。

---

**English:** Gas cavitation following the journal motion appears in ventilated (open ends) SFDs operating at low frequencies and with small to moderate journal amplitude motions. A well defined cavitation bubble containing the release of dissolved gas in the lubricant or air entrained from the vented sides follows the whirling motion of the journal; i.e. the cavitation zone appears steady in a rotating frame. The traveling gas bubble appears not to affect the generation of the squeeze film pressure in the full film zone. The persistence of this cavitation regime upon reaching steady operating conditions (high frequencies) in an aircraft application is remote.

**中文：** 随轴颈运动的气体空化（gas cavitation）出现在以低频运行、轴颈振幅为中小幅度的通风式（开口端）SFD 中。一个界限分明的空化气泡，包含润滑油中溶解气体的释放或从通风侧吸入的空气，随轴颈的涡动运动而移动；即在旋转坐标系中空化区域显得稳定。该移动的气泡似乎不影响全油膜区域内挤压油膜压力的生成。在航空应用中达到稳定工况（高频）时，这种空化状态持续存在的可能性很小。

---

**English:** Lubricant vapor cavitation appears in dampers with tight end seals that prevent entrainment of the external gas media and for operation with a sufficiently large supply pressure. In this last case, the through oil flow also prevents the ingestion of air. Furthermore, the lubricant must be relatively free of dissolved gases such as air, a condition not readily found in practice.

**中文：** 润滑油蒸气空化（lubricant vapor cavitation）出现在端部密封严密、可防止外部气体介质吸入的阻尼器中，以及供油压力足够大的运行情况下。在后一种情况下，穿流的油液也能防止空气吸入。此外，润滑油必须相对不含诸如空气等溶解气体，而这一条件在实际中并不易达到。

---

The whirl frequency and centered journal orbital amplitude equal 75 Hz and 0.180 mm, respectively. The damper radial clearance is 0.343 mm. **English:** Inevitably, the vast majority of SFDs operate with foam-like fluids considering the low values of pressure supply (small flow rate), large damper clearances, and high operating whirl frequencies. **中文：** 考虑到低供油压力（小流量）、大阻尼器间隙和高运行涡动频率，绝大多数 SFD 不可避免地以泡沫状流体运行。当然，实际中也可能出现混合运行状态。例如，严密密封的阻尼器可能同时表现出蒸气空化和空气吸入型空化，其中气泡可能共存于一个大的润滑油蒸气泡周围。注意，空气滞留会延缓油膜压力的升高，因为填充阻尼器间隙的液态润滑油较少。最终，高频运行会导致空气吸入增加，阻止任何进一步的油蒸气空化，并大幅降低 SFD 所能提供的力。

**中文：** 细致的实验表明，空气吸入和滞留会显著恶化开口端 SFD 的受迫响应 [San Andrés and Diaz, 2003]。一个简单的判据可给出阻尼器中空气吸入的可能性。供油-挤压流量参数（feed-squeeze flow parameter）γ 将润滑油供油流量 $Q_{oil}$ 与挤压油膜间隙内的动态体积变化联系起来，即

$$\gamma = \frac{Q_{oil}}{\pi D L e\omega} \tag{7}$$

**English:** If γ>1 then no air entrainment occurs, i.e. the through flow is sufficient to fill the volume change caused by the journal whirl motion. The experimental results advance an empirical correlation between γ and the amount of air entrained (volume concentration of air) in the lubricant, thus providing certainty in the modeling of the mixture. Note that $Q_{oil}$ is proportional to the difference between lubricant supply pressure and discharge pressure and to the flow conductances in the film lands and through the feed ports. The flow conductances (~1/resistances) are a function of the damper clearance and feed characteristics, lubricant and mixture viscosities, etc. Thus, air entrainment is device dependent, and its severity increases with the amplitude and frequency of journal motion. Air ingestion can be prevented by increasing the supply pressure (not practical) to ensure a sufficiently large through lubricant flow rate.

**中文：** 若 γ>1，则不会发生空气吸入，即穿流足以填充由轴颈涡动运动引起的体积变化。另一方面，当 γ<1 时将发生空气吸入和滞留。供油-挤压参数 γ 越小，阻尼器受迫性能的恶化越严重。实验结果提出了 γ 与润滑油中吸入空气量（空气体积浓度）之间的经验关联，从而为混合物建模提供了确定性。注意，$Q_{oil}$ 与润滑油供油压力和排油压力之差成正比，并与油膜面及供油孔的流动导纳（flow conductance）成正比。流动导纳（约等于 1/阻力）是阻尼器间隙、供油特性、润滑油及混合物黏度等的函数。因此，空气吸入与具体装置相关，其严重程度随轴颈运动的幅值和频率增大而增加。可以通过提高供油压力（不实用）以确保足够大的穿流润滑油流量来防止空气吸入。

## 现代挤压油膜阻尼器
## Modern squeeze film dampers

**English:** The basic design of SFDs changed little until the late 1990's when the novel wire-EDM processes allowed the construction of integral SFDs which offer distinct advantages such as reduced overall weight and length of the damper structure with less number of parts, accuracy of positioning (centering), and a split segment construction allowing easier assembly, inspection and retrofit than with any other type of damper.

**中文：** SFD 的基本设计在 20 世纪 90 年代末以前几乎没有变化，直到新颖的线切割电火花加工（wire-EDM）工艺使得整体式（integral）SFD 的制造成为可能。整体式 SFD 具有诸多独特优势，例如以更少的零件数减小阻尼器结构的整体重量和长度、定位（对中）精度高，以及分段式（split segment）结构，相比任何其他类型的阻尼器更易于装配、检查和改装。

---

**English:** Flexure pivot tilting pad bearings offer similar construction features while minimizing (assembly) stack up tolerances and avoiding pivot wear and fretting. These features are most important in aircraft engines where reduced weight and size are of utmost consideration. The integral damper, as shown in Figure 13, comprises of segmented pads instead of a fully cylindrical journal. Thin structured webs attach the inner and outer rings and perform the function of elastic supports. The thin gap between the pads and the outer ring forms the squeeze film lands. Each pad can be manufactured with a different clearance to counter the static deflection due to rotor weight. End seals restricting the axial flow through the film lands dampers provide the means to increase the damping coefficients by raising the hydrodynamic pressure in a pad film land. The series combination of a tilting pad bearing and a squeeze film damper has been implemented in numerous compressors to introduce flexibility and damping to the bearing support. The proper design of these two mechanical elements allows for the optimum damping coefficient at the bearing support and accurate relocation of the (rigid mode) rotor bearing system critical speeds away from the operating speed range. De Santiago et al. [1999] provide experimental verification and theoretical validations of the damping capability of sealed integral dampers and demonstrate the benefits of this novel technology for application in modern high performance turbomachinery.

**中文：** 挠性支点可倾瓦轴承（flexure pivot tilting pad bearing）提供了类似的结构特征，同时最大限度减小了（装配）累积公差，并避免了支点磨损和微动磨损（fretting）。这些特征在以减小重量和尺寸为首要考量的航空发动机中最为重要。如图 13 所示，整体式阻尼器由分段瓦块（segmented pad）构成，而非完整的圆柱形轴颈。薄结构腹板（web）连接内外环，起到弹性支撑的作用。瓦块与外环之间的薄间隙形成挤压油膜面。每个瓦块可制成不同间隙，以抵消由转子重量引起的静态挠度。限制油膜面轴向流动的端部密封提供了一种途径，通过提高瓦块油膜面内的流体动压来增大阻尼系数。可倾瓦轴承与挤压油膜阻尼器的串联组合已在众多压缩机中实施，以向轴承支撑引入柔性和阻尼。这两个机械元件的恰当设计可实现轴承支撑处的最优阻尼系数，并将（刚体模态）转子-轴承系统的临界转速准确地移离工作转速范围。De Santiago 等人 [1999] 提供了密封式整体阻尼器阻尼能力的实验验证和理论验证，并展示了这一新颖技术在现代高性能旋转机械应用中的优势。

## 结语
## Closure

**English:** Decades of practice demonstrate that SFDs generate the required damping even when operating with persistent air entrainment. Damper support flexibility (structural stiffness) is the key parameter that allows the intended operation in a practical application. Incidentally, the actual reduction of predicted damping at high frequencies (due to air ingestion, for example) is beneficial in rotor-bearing systems operating at supercritical speeds. However, the trend toward higher operating speeds and more stringent operating conditions demands of a reliable predictive physical model, experimentally verified.

**中文：** 数十年的实践表明，即使在持续空气吸入的情况下运行，SFD 仍能产生所需的阻尼。阻尼器支撑柔性（结构刚度）是允许在实际应用中实现预期运行的关键参数。顺便指出，高频时预测阻尼的实际降低（例如由于空气吸入）对于在超临界转速下运行的转子-轴承系统是有益的。然而，向更高运行转速和更严苛工况发展的趋势，要求有一个经实验验证的可靠预测性物理模型。

---

**English:** Childs [1993] noted that, because of lubricant cavitation and unquantifiable air ingestion, correlation between theory and experiment is less compelling for SFDs than journal bearings. In practice SFDs operate with low magnitudes of oil feed pressure (5 bar max.) that generally do not prevent the lubricant in the fluid film lands from liquid vaporization or entrainment of external gaseous media into the film lands. Open-ends SFDs are prone to develop a flow regime where the ingestion of air leads to the formation of a bubbly lubricant. Actual practice demonstrates that air ingestion greatly affects the SFD dynamic forced response.

**中文：** Childs [1993] 指出，由于润滑油空化和无法量化的空气吸入，SFD 的理论与实验之间的相关性不如滑动轴承那样令人信服。实际中 SFD 以较低的供油压力（最高 5 bar）运行，这通常无法阻止油膜面中的润滑油发生液体汽化，或阻止外部气体介质吸入油膜面。开口端 SFD 容易形成一种流动状态，其中空气吸入导致形成含气泡的（bubbly）润滑油。实际实践表明，空气吸入会极大地影响 SFD 的动态受迫响应。

---

**English:** URL http://rotorlab.tamu.edu stores digital movies recorded in a transparent squeeze film damper while operating with air entrainment¹. The movies vividly depict air ingestion and entrapment cycles with notable effects on the recorded squeeze film pressures and ensuing damper dynamic forced response.

**中文：** 网址 http://rotorlab.tamu.edu 存储了在带空气吸入运行的透明挤压油膜阻尼器中录制的数字影像¹。这些影像生动地描绘了空气吸入和滞留的循环过程，及其对所记录的挤压油膜压力和随之产生的阻尼器动态受迫响应的显著影响。

*¹ 由美国国家科学基金会（US National Science Foundation）资助，1996-2000 / Funded by US National Science Foundation, 1996-2000*

---

**English:** Most (numerical) models for prediction of finite length SFD forced response assume lubricant vapor cavitation, i.e. an operating condition likely to be found with very tight end seals or when the damper is fully submerged in a lubricant bath. Understanding of air entrainment, a pervasive phenomenon in SFDs, has just begun.

**中文：** 大多数用于预测有限长度 SFD 受迫响应的（数值）模型都假设润滑油蒸气空化，即一种很可能在端部密封极其严密或阻尼器完全浸没于润滑油浴中时出现的工况。而对 SFD 中普遍存在的空气吸入现象的理解才刚刚开始。
