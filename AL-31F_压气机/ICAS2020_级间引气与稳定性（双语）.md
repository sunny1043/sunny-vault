# Effect of Inter-stage Bleeding on Flow Stability of a Multi-stage Compressor

## 摘要 Abstract

**English:** The effect of inter-stage bleeding on flow stability of a multi-stage compressor was investigated through computational fluid dynamics (CFD) method. The bleed air is taken out at the outlet of the 2nd stage through a circumferential slot to an annular chamber, and then the bleed air is released through two axisymmetric off-take ducts. The calculation show that the surge margin as well as the matching of the multi-stage axial compressor at part speed has been improved by bleeding. There is an optimal bleed flow rate in order to get a wider operating range and higher peak efficiency (PE) under a specific corrected rotational speed. The optimal bleed flow rate increase when the corrected rotational speeds decrease. The flow mechanism of the inter-stage bleeding is the reduction of the large positive incidence angle of the first stator (S1), as well as the alleviation of the blockage of the back-stages. The mismatching of the different stages has been relieved.

**中文：** 采用计算流体动力学（computational fluid dynamics, CFD）方法研究了级间引气对多级压气机流动稳定性的影响。引气在第2级出口处通过一个周向缝（circumferential slot）引出至一个环形腔室（annular chamber），随后引气通过两个轴对称引出管道（axisymmetric off-take ducts）排出。计算结果表明，引气改善了多级轴流压气机在部分转速下的喘振裕度（surge margin）以及级间匹配。在特定换算转速下，为获得更宽的工作范围和更高的峰值效率（peak efficiency, PE），存在一个最优引气流量。随着换算转速降低，最优引气流量增大。级间引气的流动机理是减小了第1级静子（first stator, S1）的较大正攻角（positive incidence angle），同时缓解了后面各级的堵塞。各级之间的失配得到了缓解。

## 1. 引言 Introduction

**English:** The design point or matching point of a compressor is usually chosen where the compressor will operate for the most of the time or where the performance is particularly critical. This working condition is generally near the maximum pressure rise or peak efficiency of the compressor [1]. For the gas turbine engine, the machine must deliver enough pressure rise and efficiency, at the same time it must also provide sufficient surge margin at part speed, especially at the startup of the engine.

**中文：** 压气机的设计点（design point）或匹配点（matching point）通常选在压气机大部分时间运行的工况，或选在性能特别关键的工况。这一工况一般接近压气机的最大增压（maximum pressure rise）或峰值效率 [1]。对于燃气涡轮发动机而言，机器必须提供足够的增压和效率，同时还必须在部分转速下提供足够的喘振裕度，尤其是在发动机起动时。

---

**English:** At design point or matching point the incidence angle of the blade rows of the multi-stage compressor is generally near their optimal incidence. When the compressor is work at the off-design conditions, i. e. near choke or near stall at the same corrected speed or on the working line at different corrected speed, the stages matching of the multi-stage compressor will change, and the mismatching will occur. At this condition, the incidence of each blade rows cannot work at their optimal. At low part speed, the mass flow rate is at low level, the incidence angle of the front stages increase, and the operating point will move to the near stall point. For the rear stages, the annulus convergence flow path is design for the design speed. However, the pressure rise and density are lower than the design values at part speed. Thus the axial velocity at part speed will be higher than the design speed, and the incidence angle of the rear stages decrease, this will lead to the rear stages work at near choke condition [2]. In order to solve this problem, the techniques of inter-stage bleed or variable stagger stator are commonly used in the gas turbine engine to correct the incidence angle. Several arrangements of slots and bleed holes have been investigated by Leishman et al. [3] [4]. Conan et al. [5] and Wellborn et al. [6] conducted a numerical investigation of the uniform bleed on the internal flow stability of the compressor. A non-uniform bleed on the internal flow stability of the compressor through annulus cascade was conducted by Gomes et al. [7] [8]. The shape or arrangements of the bleed slots, annulus chamber, and off-take ducts all can be customized. The results of the Gomes et al. studies show that the non-uniform bleed will introduce total pressure distortion at circumferential direction. This may lead to the deterioration on the stability of the compressor. However, there are no other stages at the outlet of their annulus cascade; no further explanation is given by Gomes et al.

**中文：** 在设计点或匹配点，多级压气机各排叶片的攻角一般接近其最佳攻角（optimal incidence）。当压气机工作在非设计工况，即在相同换算转速下接近堵塞（choke）或接近失速（stall），或在不同换算转速下沿工作线运行时，多级压气机的级间匹配会发生变化，从而出现失配（mismatching）。在该工况下，各排叶片的攻角无法工作在其最优状态。在低部分转速下，质量流量处于较低水平，前面各级的攻角增大，工作点会向近失速点移动。对于后面各级，环形收缩流道是按设计转速设计的；然而在部分转速下，增压和密度低于设计值，因此部分转速下的轴向速度会高于设计转速，后面各级的攻角减小，这会导致后面各级工作在近堵塞工况 [2]。为解决这一问题，燃气涡轮发动机中通常采用级间引气（inter-stage bleed）或可调安装角静子（variable stagger stator）技术来修正攻角。Leishman 等人 [3][4] 研究了多种缝（slots）和引气孔（bleed holes）布置方案。Conan 等人 [5] 和 Wellborn 等人 [6] 对均匀引气（uniform bleed）对压气机内部流动稳定性的影响进行了数值研究。Gomes 等人 [7][8] 通过环形叶栅（annulus cascade）研究了非均匀引气（non-uniform bleed）对压气机内部流动稳定性的影响。引气缝、环形腔室和引出管道的形状或布置均可定制。Gomes 等人的研究结果表明，非均匀引气会在周向引入总压畸变（total pressure distortion），这可能导致压气机稳定性的恶化。然而，他们的环形叶栅出口处没有其他级；Gomes 等人未给出进一步的解释。

---

**English:** Through the literature review the authors find that there is little investigation on the numerical calculations on the effects of real bleed slots, bleed chamber and off-take ducts. In this paper, steady state numerical calculations have been conducted in order to get a better understanding of the effects of real bleed system structure on the internal flow stability of a multi-stage compressor.

**中文：** 通过文献综述，作者发现关于真实引气缝、引气腔室和引出管道影响的数值计算研究很少。本文进行了稳态数值计算，以更好地理解真实引气系统结构对多级压气机内部流动稳定性的影响。

Bleed air is extracted from the compressor through a bleed system, which consists of a circumferential bleed slot, an annular plenum chamber, and double axisymmetric off-take ducts. ## 3. 数值方法 Numerical Approach

**English:** The numerical calculations were performed by adopting the CFD package of ANSYS CFX. The computational meshes of the multi-stage axial compressor were generated with grid generation tool of NUMECA/IGG, the meshes were shown in Fig. 3. The total number of nodes was chosen to be about 7 million. The structured mesh of the bleed system was generated with grid generation tool of ICEM. The mesh of the off-take ducts adopted in this paper is butterfly mesh.

**中文：** 数值计算采用 ANSYS CFX 这一 CFD 软件包进行。多级轴流压气机的计算网格采用 NUMECA/IGG 网格生成工具生成，网格如图3所示。节点总数选定为约700万。引气系统的结构化网格（structured mesh）采用 ICEM 网格生成工具生成。本文引出管道采用的网格为蝶形网格（butterfly mesh）。

---

**English:** *Figure 3 – Meshes of the multi-stage axial compressor.*

**中文：** *图 3 — 多级轴流压气机的网格。* （标注了叶片排的结构化网格、第2级静子（S2）全环网格、引出管道以及蝶形网格。）

**English:** The average static pressure condition was specified at the strut outlet, mass flow rate was specified at the outlet of the bleed off-take duct (when only one of the two off-take ducts was used, the bleed off-take duct type was set as outlet, the other off-take duct boundary type switch to wall condition). The solid surfaces were prescribed as adiabatic boundaries.

**中文：** 在支板出口（strut outlet）处指定平均静压条件，在引气引出管道出口处指定质量流量（当两个引出管道中只使用其中一个时，引气引出管道类型设置为出口，另一个引出管道边界类型切换为壁面条件）。在进口边界处指定标准天（101325 Pa，288.15 K）的总压和总温。在转子/静子交界面处采用掺混面模型（mixing-plane model）。固体表面设定为绝热边界。

**English:** During the numerical calculation, the specific corrected speed line can be obtained through increasing the back pressure at the strut outlet. When the global residuals reach the convergence criteria, and the fluctuation in the inlet/outlet mass flow rate, pressure ratio, and adiabatic efficiency become stabilized, then the calculation results were considered as convergence results. The last convergence result before divergence was considered as the stall point (not the real stall) as the back pressure increasing.

**中文：** 在数值计算过程中，通过提高支板出口的背压（back pressure）可获得特定换算转速线。当全局残差达到收敛准则，且进/出口质量流量、压比和绝热效率（adiabatic efficiency）的波动趋于稳定时，计算结果即被视为收敛结果。随着背压升高，发散前最后一个收敛结果被视为失速点（并非真实失速）。

**English:** It can be seen from Fig. 5 that the change of the surge margin of the multi-stage axial compressor at 85% and 90% corrected speed is negligible. The main change at 85% and 90% corrected speed is the adiabatic efficiency as shown in Fig. 6. Through bleeding the mismatching of the stages relieved, i.e. the operating point gets a better matching. This is main reason that the adiabatic efficiency increase by bleeding off some air from the middle stages. For other part speed (Nc vary from 40% to 80%), the surge line move to the top left side of the pressure rise-mass flow characteristic. This means the surge margin has been increased significantly. Especially, when the corrected speed is lower than 70%, without bleed the working line is at left side of the surge line, by bleeding off some air the working line moves back to the right side of the surge line. The increasing of the surge margin is also related to the relief of the mismatching of the stages. It should be noted that the calculation of the efficiency with bleeding did not consider the losses introduced by the bleed; this is the main reason that the efficiency has significant difference with and without bleeding. The main considerations for adopting this efficiency calculation method is that the major function of the bleed is to improve the surge margin, in addition, the bleed operating time is short and is only used to pass the low and middle corrected speed, thus there is no need to calculate the real efficiency with bleeding.

**中文：** 由图5可见，多级轴流压气机在85%和90%换算转速下的喘振裕度变化可忽略不计。如图6所示，85%和90%换算转速下的主要变化是绝热效率。通过引气，各级的失配得到缓解，即工作点获得了更好的匹配。这是从中间各级引出部分空气后绝热效率提高的主要原因。对于其他部分转速（Nc 从40%到80%变化），喘振线向增压—流量特性的左上方移动，这意味着喘振裕度显著增加。特别是当换算转速低于70%时，无引气情况下工作线位于喘振线左侧，通过引出部分空气，工作线移回喘振线右侧。喘振裕度的增加也与各级失配的缓解有关。需要指出的是，有引气的效率计算未考虑引气引入的损失；这是有引气和无引气效率存在显著差异的主要原因。采用这种效率计算方法的主要考虑是：引气的主要功能是改善喘振裕度，此外引气工作时间短，仅用于通过中低换算转速，因此无需计算有引气时的真实效率。

### 4.2 最优引气质量流量 Optimal Bleed Mass Flow Rate

**English:** The bleed mass flow rate at different corrected rotational speed in Fig. 5 and Fig. 6 is the optimal bleed mass flow at corresponding corrected speed. A brief description of the method to get the optimal bleed mass flow at 80% corrected speed will be given in the following passage. Constant mass flow rate bleed is adopted in this paper at a specific corrected rotational speed. The trends of surge margin and efficiency along with the bleed mass flow rate are shown in Fig. 7 and Fig. 8. There are many different ways of defining surge margin, two of the most commonly used definitions are adopted in this paper. One of the simplest definitions is defined in Eq. (1).

**中文：** 图5和图6中不同换算转速下的引气质量流量是对应换算转速下的最优引气质量流量。下文将简要说明获取80%换算转速下最优引气质量流量的方法。本文在特定换算转速下采用恒定质量流量引气。喘振裕度和效率随引气质量流量的变化趋势如图7和图8所示。喘振裕度有许多不同的定义方式，本文采用了两种最常用的定义。其中最简单的一种定义如式(1)所示。

---

$$\mathrm{SM}_{pr} = \frac{\left(\mathrm{PR}_{S} - \mathrm{PR}_{W}\right)}{\mathrm{PR}_{W}} \times 100\% \tag{1}$$

**English:** Where PR denotes pressure ratio, subscript 'S' and 'W' denote the operating point at surge and working point respectively.

**中文：** 式中 PR 表示压比，下标 'S' 和 'W' 分别表示喘振点和工作点的运行状态。

---

**English:** The other commonly used definition of the surge margin as defined by Cumpsty [1] is given by Eq. (2).

**中文：** 另一种常用的喘振裕度定义由 Cumpsty [1] 给出，如式(2)所示。

---

$$\mathrm{SM} = \left[1 - \left(\frac{\mathrm{PR}_{W}}{\mathrm{PR}_{S}} \times \frac{G_{c:S}}{G_{c:W}}\right)\right] \times 100\% \tag{2}$$

**English:** Where Gc:S and Gc:W denote corrected mass flow rate at surge and working operating point respectively.

**中文：** 式中 G_{c:S} 和 G_{c:W} 分别表示喘振点和工作点的换算质量流量。

---

**English:** *Figure 7 – Trends of the surge margin along with the bleed mass flow rate.*

**中文：** *图 7 — 喘振裕度随引气质量流量的变化趋势。* （横坐标为引气流量/进口流量（bleedmass/inletmass），纵坐标为喘振裕度；给出 SM_pr 和 SM 两条曲线，二者均随引气量先增后略降，约在10%处达到最大。）

---

**English:** *Figure 8 – Trend of the efficiency along with the bleed mass flow rate.*

**中文：** *图 8 — 效率随引气质量流量的变化趋势。* （横坐标为引气流量/进口流量，纵坐标为归一化峰值效率 η_pc/η_ref；峰值效率随引气量增加而上升，约在10%处达到最大。）

---

**English:** At 0% mass flow rate, i. e. without bleeding, the surge margin SMpr and SM is low, as the bleed mass flow rate increase, the surge margin SMpr and SM also increase. When the bleed mass flow reaches about 10% inlet mass flow, the SM reaches its maximum value. The variation of SM along the bleed mass flow rate is thought to be caused by the changing of the stages matching, at lower bleed flow rate, the mismatching of the stages cannot be relieved. This is also consistent with the change of the incidence angle with the mass flow. At part speed, the front stages tend to operate near the stall point, while the rear stages tend to operate near the choke point. As the bleed mass flow increase, the incidence of the front stages will be reduced, the operating point of the front stage will move to its optimum incidence, the surge margin increase. As the bleed mass flow continues to increase, the incidence will decrease to a negative incidence, and the operating point will move to the choking point, the surge margin will decrease. Therefore, there is an optimal bleed mass flow in order to get maximum surge margin. It can be seen from Fig. 7 that the maximum value of the SMpr and SM do not have the same bleed mass flow rate, in order to decide which surge margin is the better one, the peak efficiency along the bleed mass is illustrated in Fig. 8. It can be seen that the bleed mass flow is also about 10% when the efficiency reaches its maximum, this bleed mass flow is consistent with the optimal mass flow of the SM. Therefore, the surge margin definition SM is chosen as the suitable definition. The optimal bleed mass flow rate at different corrected rotational speed is shown in Fig. 9. It can be seen that the lower the corrected speed; the greater the amount of bleed mass flow is needed to be extracted from the middle stage.

**中文：** 在0%质量流量（即无引气）时，喘振裕度 SM_pr 和 SM 较低；随着引气质量流量增加，喘振裕度 SM_pr 和 SM 也随之增加。当引气质量流量达到进口质量流量的约10%时，SM 达到最大值。SM 随引气质量流量的变化被认为是由级间匹配的变化引起的——在较低引气流量下，各级的失配无法得到缓解。这也与攻角随流量的变化相一致。在部分转速下，前面各级倾向于在近失速点运行，而后面各级倾向于在近堵塞点运行。随着引气质量流量增加，前面各级的攻角减小，前级工作点向其最佳攻角移动，喘振裕度增加。当引气质量流量继续增大时，攻角会减小至负攻角（negative incidence），工作点向堵塞点移动，喘振裕度将下降。因此，存在一个最优引气质量流量以获得最大喘振裕度。由图7可见，SM_pr 和 SM 的最大值并不出现在相同的引气质量流量处；为判断哪种喘振裕度更优，图8给出了峰值效率随引气量的变化。可以看出，效率达到最大时的引气质量流量也约为10%，该引气质量流量与 SM 的最优流量一致。因此，选择喘振裕度定义 SM 作为合适的定义。不同换算转速下的最优引气质量流量如图9所示。可以看出，换算转速越低，需要从中间级引出的引气质量流量越大。

**中文：** *图 9 — 不同换算转速下的最优引气质量流量。* （横坐标为换算转速 Nc（40%~90%），纵坐标为引气流量/进口流量；分别给出工作点、堵塞点、峰值效率点对应的最优引气量，三者均随换算转速降低而线性增大。）

### 4.3 级特性与流场分析 Stage Characteristic and Flow-Field Analysis

**English:** In order to get a clearer picture of the variation of the operating point at part speed, the stage loading-flow coefficient characteristic of the compressor at 80% corrected speed is given in Fig. 10. The flow coefficient is defined in Eq. (3).

**中文：** 为更清晰地了解部分转速下工作点的变化，图10给出了80%换算转速下压气机的级负荷—流量系数特性。流量系数（flow coefficient）定义如式(3)所示。

---

$$\phi = \frac{V_x}{U_{tip}} \tag{3}$$

**English:** Where Vx denotes the axial velocity and Utip is the blade tip speed.

**中文：** 式中 V_x 表示轴向速度，U_{tip} 为叶尖速度。

---

**English:** The stage loading is defined in Eq. (4).

**中文：** 级负荷（stage loading）定义如式(4)所示。

---

$$\psi = \frac{\Delta h}{U_{tip}^{2}} \tag{4}$$

**English:** Where Δh denotes the enthalpy change, and Utip is the blade tip speed.

**中文：** 式中 Δh 表示焓变，U_{tip} 为叶尖速度。

---

**English:** It can be seen that without bleeding the front stages (1st and 2nd stage) have the tendency to move towards stall; and the rear stages (3rd and 4th stage) have the tendency to move to the choke or negative incidence stall, and the compressor becomes mismatched. With some air extracted from the middle stage, the front stages move away from the stall, and the rear stages move away from choke, and the compressor moves back to the matching point.

**中文：** 可以看出，无引气时前面各级（第1级和第2级）有向失速移动的趋势，而后面各级（第3级和第4级）有向堵塞或负攻角失速移动的趋势，压气机变得失配。从中间级引出部分空气后，前面各级远离失速，后面各级远离堵塞，压气机回到匹配点。

## 5. 结论 Conclusions

**English:** In this paper, the effect of inter-stage bleeding on flow stability of a multi-stage axial compressor has been investigated through steady numerical calculations. Several conclusions have been drawn from this paper:

**中文：** 本文通过稳态数值计算研究了级间引气对多级轴流压气机流动稳定性的影响。本文得出了以下若干结论：

---

**English:** (1) The surge margin of the multi-stage axial compressor has been increased through inter-stage bleeding at part speed.

**中文：** （1）通过部分转速下的级间引气，多级轴流压气机的喘振裕度得到提高。

---

**English:** (2) There is an optimal bleed mass flow rate at a specific corrected speed in order to get a maximum surge margin.

**中文：** （2）在特定换算转速下，为获得最大喘振裕度，存在一个最优引气质量流量。

---

**English:** (3) The optimal bleed mass flow at different corrected speed changes with the corrected speed; the lower the speed the higher the bleed mass flow rate is needed.

**中文：** （3）不同换算转速下的最优引气质量流量随换算转速变化；转速越低，所需的引气质量流量越高。

---

**English:** (4) The mechanism of surge margin increase with inter-stage bleed is related to the decrease of the incidence angle of the S1, and the relieved of the blockage at the back-stages. The mismatching of the stages has been relieved.

**中文：** （4）级间引气提高喘振裕度的机理与第1级静子（S1）攻角的减小以及后面各级堵塞的缓解有关。各级之间的失配得到了缓解。
