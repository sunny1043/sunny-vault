## 摘要 Abstract

**English:** Numerical investigation of an axial flow rotor is carried out for its performance characterization and aerodynamic behavior during the design and off-design operating conditions. The study focuses on capturing the transonic flow features from the choke point (CP) to the near stall (NS) point in the rotor. This includes the analysis of passage shock structure, its movement in the blade passage with varying back-pressure, shock boundary layer interaction, tip leakage flow structure, and resulting losses. The study is carried out from 60 % to 100 % of the design speed using steady and unsteady RANS simulations. Three turbulence models, namely; SST, k-ε, and Reynolds stress models, are employed. The SST model predicted the closest approximation to the experimental data. The rotor aerodynamic performance is predicted in terms of total pressure ratio, efficiency, and flow contours. Unsteady analysis revealed that the primary and secondary tip leakage vortices, combined with the suction side tip corner separation, are the major instabilities near the stall region.

**中文：** 本文对一台轴流转子进行了数值研究，以表征其在设计与非设计工况下的性能与气动行为。研究重点在于捕捉转子从堵塞点（choke point, CP）到近失速点（near stall, NS）的跨声速流动特征，包括通道激波（passage shock）结构、其在叶片通道内随反压（back-pressure）变化的移动、激波边界层干涉（shock boundary layer interaction）、叶尖泄漏流（tip leakage flow）结构以及由此产生的损失。研究覆盖设计转速的 60 % 至 100 %，采用定常与非定常雷诺平均纳维-斯托克斯（RANS）模拟。文中采用了三种湍流模型，即 SST、k-ε 和雷诺应力模型（Reynolds stress model）。SST 模型给出了与实验数据最接近的预测结果。转子的气动性能以总压比、效率和流动云图的形式给出。非定常分析揭示：主、次叶尖泄漏涡（primary and secondary tip leakage vortices）与吸力侧叶尖角区分离（suction side tip corner separation）相结合，是近失速区域的主要失稳源。

## 1. 引言 Introduction

**English:** High-speed compressor rotors have the potential to generate higher pressure ratio and efficiency with a minimum number of stages enabling significant weight reduction. However, the rotor speed is constrained by the strong shock structures encountered in the blade passage. The strong shock system, in combination with tip leakage flow (TLF), is of significant concern within the gas turbine fraternity, as it contributes to around 30 % of the passage losses¹⁻³. The interaction of tip leakage flow with the incoming core flow is one of the prominent sources of the stall inception in the tip critical rotor⁴⁻⁵. Therefore, understanding their genesis is crucial to curtailing the passage secondary losses and tip leakage flow.

**中文：** 高速压气机转子有潜力以最少的级数产生更高的压比和效率，从而显著减轻重量。然而，转子的转速受限于叶片通道内出现的强激波结构。强激波系统与叶尖泄漏流（TLF）相结合，是燃气涡轮领域高度关注的问题，因为它贡献了约 30 % 的通道损失¹⁻³。叶尖泄漏流与来流核心流的相互作用，是叶尖临界转子（tip critical rotor）中失速起始（stall inception）的主要来源之一⁴⁻⁵。因此，理解它们的成因对于抑制通道二次损失和叶尖泄漏流至关重要。

---

**English:** There were enormous efforts put in by many researchers to suppress the dominance of passage shock and TLF, such as; Sunder⁶⁻⁷, et al. investigated the effect of TLF and passage shock interaction on rotor performance. The study showed the blockage in the tip region is essentially due to the blade boundary layer separation caused by passage shock and TLF interaction. Gerolymos⁸, et al. numerically analyzed the tip clearance flow and consequent secondary losses using various turbulence models. It was found that the overall performance prediction was well matched with experiments; however, hub corner separation and tip mixing regions were difficult to capture numerically. Hah⁹, et al. carried out the unsteady analysis of the near casing flow of a forward-swept transonic rotor. The study was focused on analyzing the role of TLF, passage shock, and casing boundary layer interaction in stall inception. It was reported that tip leakage vortices do not break despite the rotor approaching stall, and the flow field unsteadiness was majorly due to the shock oscillations. Contrary to this, the study by Yamada¹⁰, et al. showed that tip leakage vortices do break due to interaction with the passage shock and create significant flow blockage near the tip region.

**中文：** 众多研究者为抑制通道激波与叶尖泄漏流的主导作用付出了巨大努力。例如，Sunder⁶⁻⁷ 等人研究了 TLF 与通道激波相互作用对转子性能的影响。研究表明，叶尖区域的堵塞本质上源于通道激波与 TLF 相互作用引起的叶片边界层分离。Gerolymos⁸ 等人采用多种湍流模型对叶尖间隙流及由此产生的二次损失进行了数值分析。结果发现整体性能预测与实验吻合良好；然而，轮毂角区分离（hub corner separation）和叶尖掺混区难以通过数值方法捕捉。Hah⁹ 等人对前掠跨声速转子近机匣流动进行了非定常分析，研究聚焦于 TLF、通道激波与机匣边界层相互作用在失速起始中的作用。研究指出，尽管转子接近失速，叶尖泄漏涡并未破碎，流场的非定常性主要由激波振荡引起。与此相反，Yamada¹⁰ 等人的研究表明，叶尖泄漏涡因与通道激波相互作用而破碎，并在叶尖区域附近造成显著的流动堵塞。

---

**English:** Hah¹¹, et al. investigated the 3D shock structure, shock boundary layer interaction, radial mixing, wake development, and flow separation in a transonic rotor. The study reported that the shock system induced flow separation above 60 % of the blade span, and below 60 %, the separation was due to the usual pressure gradient. In the separation region, the strong radially outward motion of the flow was also reported. Biollo¹², et al. explored the effect of shock/boundary layer interaction in a three-dimensional stacked rotor. It was found that the new rotor had higher efficiency and the same pressure ratio compared to the baseline one. Modified stacking helped to reduce the shock losses and improved the flow field in the end wall region.

**中文：** Hah¹¹ 等人研究了跨声速转子中的三维激波结构、激波边界层干涉、径向掺混、尾迹发展和流动分离。研究指出，激波系统在叶片展向 60 % 以上诱发流动分离，而在 60 % 以下，分离则源于常规的压力梯度。在分离区内，还观察到流动强烈的径向向外运动。Biollo¹² 等人探究了三维积叠转子（3D stacked rotor）中激波/边界层干涉的影响。结果发现，与基准转子相比，新转子具有更高的效率和相同的压比。改进的积叠方式有助于减小激波损失，并改善端壁区域的流场。

---

**English:** Yongzhen¹³, et al. employed a Shock Control Bump (SCB) on the suction surface of a transonic rotor. It was observed that above 95 % of design speed, SCB breaks the strong shock into two weak shocks and reduces the flow separation; however, SCB was not very effective below design speed. Juan¹⁴, et al. studied the flow structure within the tip region of a transonic rotor capturing 2D and 3D shock structure, expansion wave around the leading edge, and tip leakage flow. The study pointed out two observations; (i) There is an interface between incoming core flow and TLF, and (ii) Based on blade loading, there exist two components of TLF along the blade chord, and each of them contributes differently to stall inception.

**中文：** Yongzhen¹³ 等人在跨声速转子吸力面上采用了激波控制凸包（Shock Control Bump, SCB）。观察发现，在设计转速 95 % 以上时，SCB 可将强激波分解为两道弱激波并减小流动分离；然而在设计转速以下，SCB 效果不佳。Juan¹⁴ 等人研究了跨声速转子叶尖区域内的流动结构，捕捉了二维和三维激波结构、前缘附近的膨胀波以及叶尖泄漏流。研究指出两点观察：（i）来流核心流与 TLF 之间存在一个界面；（ii）依据叶片载荷，TLF 沿叶片弦向存在两个分量，且各自对失速起始的贡献不同。

---

**English:** Zinon¹⁵ extensively assessed the effectiveness of various turbulence models for predicting transitional flows on a double circular arch compressor blade. Based on the outcome, it was found that for accurate prediction of complex flow fields encountered in compressors, unsteady Reynolds averaged Navier Stoke or hybrid simulation having an unsteady approach is required.

**中文：** Zinon¹⁵ 广泛评估了多种湍流模型在预测双圆弧（double circular arc）压气机叶片转捩流动方面的有效性。结果发现，为准确预测压气机中遇到的复杂流场，需要采用非定常雷诺平均纳维-斯托克斯方法或具有非定常处理的混合模拟方法。

This transonic rotor is part of a research compressor stage at the Propulsion Division, CSIR-NAL¹⁶⁻¹⁷. The technical details are given in Table 1.

**中文：** 本研究沿着类似方向，旨在研究轴流转子中的跨声速流动特征，如激波结构、叶尖泄漏流和激波边界层干涉。该跨声速转子是 CSIR-NAL 推进部（Propulsion Division）一台研究用压气机级的组成部分¹⁶⁻¹⁷。其技术细节列于表 1。

## 4. 转子流动特征 Rotor Flow Features

**English:** The various flow features of the rotor for CP, PE, and NS points at the hub, mean, and tip sections are presented with the help of relative Mach (Mrel) contours in Fig. 5(a), (b), and (c) and are marked with the white dashed line in the respective figures. At the choke point, the blade incidence is almost zero; therefore, as the flow approaches the blade leading edge, the streamline gets deflected around the blade Pressure Surface (PS) and Suction Surface (SS). At this point, neither the blades are fully loaded, nor the back pressure is very high; hence the flow accelerates in the blade passage leading to a supersonic Mach number upon the blade surface on either surface. This results in the formation of strong shocks on the Suction Surface (SS) and Pressure Surface (PS). The shock Boundary Layer (BL) interaction at the suction surface makes the flow separate from the surface. The pressure rise across the shock is so high that it can cause the boundary layer to separate a little upstream of the shock. Consequently, there is a sudden change in the direction of the core flow, accompanied by an oblique shock interacting with the main shock and creating a lambda shock structure. The pressure surface shock boundary layer interaction does not cause flow separation; this is attributed to the weak interaction.

## 5. 转子近失速的非定常分析 Unsteady Analysis of the Rotor Near Stall

**English:** The unsteady simulation of the rotor flow physics is carried out at the NS point. The steady-state RANS solution corresponding to the NS point is taken as the initial value. Ten rotor revolutions are defined in the simulation. The single-blade passing period is calculated as the time taken by the rotor blade to pass the one pitch of the stator blade. Each passing period is divided into 20-time steps corresponding to a size of 1.19×10⁻⁵ seconds. The total time steps for the ten revolutions are 4200, which is found to be adequate to achieve a converged solution. The time history of the static pressure signal is monitored, and as the periodicity of the pressure signal becomes repetitive in nature, the solution is considered to be converged. Figure 10 shows the static pressure signals captured at the blade trailing edge and the corresponding Fourier transform, giving the first, second, and third harmonics of the Blade Passing Frequency (BPF). Here the frequency is non-dimensionalized by the 4638 Hz, which corresponds to the rotor BPF.

**中文：** 转子流动物理的非定常模拟在 NS 点进行。以 NS 点对应的定常 RANS 解作为初值。模拟中定义了十个转子转数。单叶片通过周期定义为转子叶片通过一个静子叶片节距所需的时间。每个通过周期被划分为 20 个时间步，对应时间步长 1.19×10⁻⁵ 秒。十转的总时间步数为 4200，发现足以获得收敛解。监测静压信号的时间历程，当压力信号的周期性变为重复性时，认为求解收敛。图 10 给出了在叶片尾缘捕捉的静压信号及其对应的傅里叶变换，给出了叶片通过频率（Blade Passing Frequency, BPF）的一、二、三阶谐波。此处频率以对应转子 BPF 的 4638 Hz 进行无量纲化。

**English:** The dominant vortex structures in the rotor passage before the onset of stall are Primary Tip Leakage Vortex (P-TLV), Secondary Tip Leakage Vortex (S-TLV), vortex due to Shock Boundary Layer Interaction (SBL-V), leading-edge spillage vortex (SV), suction side corner vortex (H-CV) at the hub, Horseshoe Vortex (HSV) near the hub leading edge, ramp vortex due to hub contour and tip corner vortex, all of them are marked in Fig. 11. Most of these vortex structures are inherent; however, their frequency and magnitude depend on the blade passing frequency and downstream back pressure for a particular speed and inlet conditions. The primary sources of rotor tip instability are; tip leakage vortex (TLV), tip corner vortices (T-CV), and other secondary flow structures. At stable operating conditions, TLV is confined in the close vicinity of the blade, creating one single oval-shaped vortex in the passage (Fig 11(a). With increasing back pressure, the TLV grows in size, and it becomes unstable to an extent where it starts breaking into two vortices (Fig. 11(b) and (c)). From this point, any incremental rise in the back pressure causes the breaking of the primary P-TLV. The S-TLV also interacts with the tip corner vortices (T-CV) near the trailing edge (trajectory shown by the red dotted line Fig. 11(e) and (f)). The primary P-TLV and the S-TLV, along with T-CV, get mixed near the blade trailing edge and break into multiple vortex structures, creating around 30 % blockage at the rotor exit. This eventually leads the rotor into the stall region.

**中文：** 失速起始前转子通道内的主导涡结构包括：主叶尖泄漏涡（Primary Tip Leakage Vortex, P-TLV）、次叶尖泄漏涡（Secondary Tip Leakage Vortex, S-TLV）、激波边界层干涉所致涡（SBL-V）、前缘溢流涡（leading-edge spillage vortex, SV）、轮毂处吸力侧角区涡（suction side corner vortex, H-CV）、轮毂前缘附近的马蹄涡（Horseshoe Vortex, HSV）、由轮毂型线引起的斜坡涡（ramp vortex）以及叶尖角区涡，所有这些均标注于图 11。这些涡结构大多是固有的；但在特定转速和进口条件下，其频率和量级取决于叶片通过频率和下游反压。转子叶尖失稳的主要来源是：叶尖泄漏涡（TLV）、叶尖角区涡（tip corner vortices, T-CV）以及其他二次流结构。在稳定工况下，TLV 被限制在叶片附近，在通道内形成单个椭圆形涡（图 11(a)）。随着反压增大，TLV 尺寸增大，并失稳到开始破碎为两个涡的程度（图 11(b) 和 (c)）。从此点起，反压的任何进一步上升都会引起主涡 P-TLV 的破碎。S-TLV 还在尾缘附近与叶尖角区涡（T-CV）相互作用（其轨迹由图 11(e) 和 (f) 中的红色点线示出）。主涡 P-TLV 与 S-TLV 连同 T-CV 在叶片尾缘附近掺混并破碎为多个涡结构，在转子出口造成约 30 % 的堵塞。这最终使转子进入失速区域。

## 6. 结论 Conclusions

**English:** Flow physics of a transonic rotor is investigated using a steady and unsteady Reynolds averaged Navier Stokes (RANS) solver. The analysis is carried out for various speeds and focused on choke point (CP), peak efficiency (PE) point, and near stall (NS). Out of SST, k-ε, and Reynolds stress models, the SST model gave the closest approximation to the experimental data specifically predicting the stall point mass flow (2 % difference in stall mass flow).

**中文：** 采用定常与非定常雷诺平均纳维-斯托克斯（RANS）求解器研究了跨声速转子的流动物理。分析在多种转速下进行，重点关注堵塞点（CP）、峰值效率（PE）点和近失速（NS）点。在 SST、k-ε 和雷诺应力模型中，SST 模型给出了与实验数据最接近的近似，尤其是在预测失速点质量流量方面（失速质量流量差异为 2 %）。

---

**English:** At transonic speed, the shock boundary layer interaction in combination with TLF creates higher losses in the tip region. The band of shock movement in the upstream direction is between 45 % to 18 % from CP to NS. The tip shock structure is very complex at the choke point due to the formation of lambda shock, whereas at PE and NS only a normal shock is formed. At 100 % of design speed, the span-wise (radially down) dominance of supersonic flow is 40 %, 25 %, and 30 % for CP, PE, and NS, which causes the occurrence of shock and consequent flow separation. Leading edge acceleration from the hub (radially up) has caused a mild shock and resulted in separation and reattachment over the suction surface. The radial migration of flow near the hub is observed due to corner separation, which becomes significant at the NS. The interference of the TLV with the adjacent blade is not observed for CP and PE; however, it has significant interference with the adjacent blade pressure surface for NS. The pitch-wise total pressure losses are also large at CP and NS due to the combined effect of TLF and unstable casing boundary layer.

**中文：** 在跨声速时，激波边界层干涉与 TLF 相结合在叶尖区域造成更高的损失。从 CP 到 NS，激波向上游移动的范围在弦长的 45 % 至 18 % 之间。叶尖激波结构在堵塞点处因 λ 激波的形成而非常复杂，而在 PE 和 NS 点处仅形成一道正激波。在设计转速的 100 % 时，超声速流的展向（径向向下）主导范围在 CP、PE 和 NS 工况下分别为 40 %、25 % 和 30 %，这引起激波出现及随之而来的流动分离。来自轮毂的前缘加速（径向向上）引起一道弱激波，导致吸力面上的分离与再附。由于角区分离，观察到轮毂附近流动的径向迁移，该现象在 NS 工况下变得显著。在 CP 和 PE 工况下未观察到 TLV 对相邻叶片的干扰；但在 NS 工况下，它对相邻叶片压力面有显著干扰。由于 TLF 与不稳定机匣边界层的共同作用，CP 和 NS 工况下的周向总压损失也很大。

---

**English:** The unsteady analysis showed that the major source of rotor tip instabilities is primary and secondary tip leakage vortices. These vortices get mixed with the suction side tip corner separation and break into multiple numbers of small vortices. Around 30 % of blockage is created by the vortex breaking near the stall condition. Vortex structures near the hub such as ramp vortex (RV), horseshoe vortex (HSV), and suction side corner vortex near the hub (H-CV) grow very marginally with the onset of the stall and do not cause any severe flow instability.

**中文：** 非定常分析表明，转子叶尖失稳的主要来源是主、次叶尖泄漏涡。这些涡与吸力侧叶尖角区分离掺混并破碎为多个小涡。在近失速工况下，涡破碎造成约 30 % 的堵塞。轮毂附近的涡结构，如斜坡涡（RV）、马蹄涡（HSV）和轮毂附近吸力侧角区涡（H-CV），在失速起始时仅极小幅度增长，不引起任何严重的流动失稳。
