# NASA 航空推进技术的影响与前景
# Impact and Promise of NASA Aeropropulsion Technology

> **作者 Authors:** Neal T. Saunders 与 David N. Bowditch
> **来源 Source:** NASA 刘易斯研究中心（NASA Lewis Research Center），报告编号 N92-22511
> **由 NASA 技术报告服务器（NASA Technical Reports Server）提供**

## 摘要
## Summary

**English:** The aeropropulsion industry in the United States has established an enviable record of leading the world in aeropropulsion for commercial and military aircraft. NASA's aeropropulsion propulsion program (primarily conducted through the Lewis Research Center) has significantly contributed to that success through research and technology advances and technology demonstrations. Some past NASA contributions to engines in current aircraft are reviewed, and technologies emerging from current research programs for the aircraft of the 1990's are described. Finally, current program thrusts toward improving propulsion systems in the 2000's for subsonic commercial aircraft and higher speed aircraft such as the High-Speed Civil Transport and the National Aerospace Plane (NASP) are discussed.

**中文：** 美国的航空推进工业（aeropropulsion industry）在为商用和军用飞机提供航空推进方面建立了令世人羡慕的世界领先记录。NASA 的航空推进计划（主要通过刘易斯研究中心（Lewis Research Center）开展）通过研究、技术进步和技术演示，为这一成功作出了重大贡献。本文回顾了 NASA 过去对现役飞机发动机的一些贡献，并介绍了从当前研究计划中涌现、面向 20 世纪 90 年代飞机的技术。最后，本文讨论了当前计划中面向 21 世纪、旨在改进亚声速商用飞机以及高速民用运输机（High-Speed Civil Transport）和国家空天飞机（National Aerospace Plane, NASP）等更高速飞机推进系统的研究方向。

## NASA Aeropropulsion Contributions

### 基础研究与技术计划
### Base Research and Technology Program

**English:** Fifty years after the Whittle engine first ran, it is interesting to review the improvement in efficiency of commercial turbofan engines, shown in figure 2. The thermal efficiency of the Whittle engine was relatively low because of its low pressure ratio and low maximum temperature. As improved materials and aerodynamics became available, these parameters increased dramatically, improving the core thermal efficiency for first-generation turbine engines. Second- and third-generation turbine engines benefited from further increases in thermal efficiency and also obtained major improvements in propulsive efficiency by increasing the bypass ratio of the turbofan engine. Advanced turboprop engines will obtain further dramatic increases in propulsive efficiency by increasing the bypass ratio to its ultimate practical value.

**中文：** 在惠特尔发动机首次运转 50 年后，回顾商用涡扇发动机效率的提升颇为有趣，如图 2 所示。惠特尔发动机的热效率（thermal efficiency）相对较低，因为其压比（pressure ratio）和最高温度都很低。随着改进的材料和气动技术的出现，这些参数显著提高，使第一代涡轮发动机的核心热效率（core thermal efficiency）得到改善。第二代和第三代涡轮发动机不仅在热效率上获得进一步提升，还通过提高涡扇发动机的涵道比（bypass ratio）在推进效率（propulsive efficiency）方面取得重大改进。先进涡桨发动机将通过把涵道比提高到其实际可行的极限值，使推进效率获得进一步的显著提升。

**English:** Base research and technology contributions of NASA's aeropropulsion program have been many and varied, and some of the more significant technologies are listed in figure 3. **English:** Ceramic thermal barrier coatings are being used in many of today's engines to extend the lives of metal parts used in combustors and turbines. The use of thermal barrier coatings by applying a ceramic coating onto a metal burner or turbine part to protect the metal and reduce its temperature was recognized in the 1960's. However, early applications were frustrated by the coating spalling off after short periods of use. NASA led research on those coatings which identified the failure mode as oxidation of the underlying metal and developed bond coatings and application procedures that have successfully prevented the coating failure. Increased reliability has resulted in the increased use of coatings in engines during the past two decades (fig. 5). Initial use was limited to a band-aiding approach to extend the life of combustor liners (left). During the 1980's improved thermal barrier coatings have been applied to selected areas, such as turbine vane platforms (center), to extend life and reduce cooling air requirements. As the technology of these coatings reaches full maturity, thermal barrier coatings will be extended to more critical areas, such as the aerodynamic surfaces of turbine blades (right).

**中文：** 陶瓷热障涂层（ceramic thermal barrier coating）正被用于当今许多发动机中，以延长燃烧室和涡轮所用金属部件的寿命。通过在金属燃烧器或涡轮部件上施加陶瓷涂层来保护金属并降低其温度，这种热障涂层的应用在 20 世纪 60 年代即已被认识到。然而，早期的应用因涂层在短期使用后剥落（spalling）而受挫。NASA 主导了对这些涂层的研究，确定其失效模式为底层金属的氧化，并开发出粘结涂层（bond coating）和涂覆工艺，成功防止了涂层失效。可靠性的提高使涂层在过去二十年中在发动机上的应用日益增多（图 5）。最初的应用仅限于一种"打补丁"式的方法，以延长燃烧室火焰筒（combustor liner）的寿命（左）。在 20 世纪 80 年代，改进的热障涂层被应用于特定区域，例如涡轮导向叶片缘板（turbine vane platform）（中），以延长寿命并减少冷却空气需求。随着这些涂层技术完全成熟，热障涂层将扩展到更关键的区域，例如涡轮叶片的气动表面（右）。

**English:** The trend in turbine engines toward more controlled variables to get improved performance and efficiency, as shown in figure 7, has made it necessary to switch from the old reliable hydromechanical control to a modern, flexible digital control. The many technical barriers to this transition have been a subject of research at Lewis since a J-85 engine was first operated under digital control in 1970. To develop a formalized procedure to design controls for so many simultaneous variables, multivariable control theory for turbine engines was developed by Lewis and demonstrated in the F100 Multivariable Control Program. Since the most unreliable components of the control are the engine sensors, Lewis developed algorithms to allow the failure of sensors to be detected and accommodated while continuing to safely control the engine. Digital control is currently being used on the F100 in a supervisory trimming mode with a hydromechanical control and is being demonstrated in flight in a full authority mode in the digital electronic engine control (DEEC) for the F100 engine. This type of control system will be on most of the new high-performance engines of the 1990's and 2000's.

**中文：** 如图 7 所示，涡轮发动机为获得更高性能和效率而趋向于控制更多变量，这使得有必要从可靠的老式液压机械控制（hydromechanical control）转向现代化、灵活的数字控制（digital control）。自 1970 年一台 J-85 发动机首次在数字控制下运转以来，这一转变面临的诸多技术障碍一直是刘易斯的研究课题。为建立一套设计如此众多同时变量控制系统的规范化程序，刘易斯开发了涡轮发动机的多变量控制理论（multivariable control theory），并在 F100 多变量控制计划中进行了演示。由于控制系统中最不可靠的部件是发动机传感器，刘易斯开发了相关算法，使传感器的故障能够被检测并加以补偿，同时继续安全地控制发动机。数字控制目前在 F100 上以监督修正模式（supervisory trimming mode）与液压机械控制配合使用，并正在 F100 发动机的数字电子发动机控制系统（digital electronic engine control, DEEC）中以全权限模式（full authority mode）进行飞行演示。这类控制系统将装备于 20 世纪 90 年代和 21 世纪初的大多数新型高性能发动机上。

### 先进涡桨计划
### Advanced Turboprop Program

**English:** The effect of bypass ratio on civil transport engines in terms of fuel usage is shown in figure 8. The specific fuel consumption (SFC) of turbine engines has declined as the bypass ratio has been increased. When turbine engines were first introduced in commercial service, they were a great success as long as the fuel price was very low. To improve fuel efficiency, the bypass ratio was first increased to about 2 for the low-bypass engine and finally to about 7 for the high-bypass engines with resulting large decreases in SFC. When the fuel crisis hit in the mid-1970's, it became apparent that further decreases in SFC were very desirable. Therefore, the further reduction in SFC available with the ultimate in high bypass, the propeller, was recognized as a goal worth investigating.

**中文：** 涵道比对民用运输机发动机燃油消耗的影响如图 8 所示。随着涵道比的增大，涡轮发动机的耗油率（specific fuel consumption, SFC）不断下降。当涡轮发动机首次投入商业使用时，只要燃油价格非常低廉，它们就取得了巨大成功。为提高燃油效率，涵道比首先被提高到约 2（低涵道比发动机），最终提高到约 7（高涵道比发动机），从而使耗油率大幅下降。当 20 世纪 70 年代中期燃油危机爆发时，人们清楚地认识到进一步降低耗油率是非常必要的。因此，借助高涵道比的极致形式——螺旋桨（propeller）——所能实现的进一步降低耗油率，被认定为一个值得研究的目标。

**English:** *Figure 8.* — High-bypass engines have better subsonic performance.

**中文：** *图 8* — 高涵道比发动机具有更好的亚声速性能（纵轴为相对耗油率 SFC，横轴为年份，依次展示涡喷、低涵道比涡扇、高涵道比涡扇和桨扇（propfan），桨扇相比可再降低约 40%）。

**English:** As a result of earlier turboprop experience, it was initially difficult to get the reintroduction of propellers for propulsion of commercial aircraft taken seriously. To compete with turbofan aircraft, similar speed and cabin comfort were required. Therefore, as shown in figure 9, the initial research at Lewis in the late 1970's was aimed at demonstrating desirable performance and noise at higher cruise speeds than had previously been obtained with propellers. The success of this research indicated the feasibility of achieving major reductions in fuel usage with advanced propellers. However, studies and analyses indicated that the full turboprop system had to be demonstrated in flight tests. So research in mechanical systems, such as gearing and pitch-change mechanisms, and propulsion integration were initiated in the early 1980's. While single-rotation propeller systems are simpler and have application to many types of aircraft, it was recognized that the swirl loss of about 8 percent could be avoided with counterrotation. The additional benefit of propulsion integration on aircraft with tail-mounted engines further increased interest in counterrotation so that research was started in the mid-1980's. These programs have culminated in flight demonstrations of the technology by Lockheed on a Gulfstream II, by Boeing on a 727, and by McDonnell Douglas on an MD90. NASA has been actively involved as a partner in all of these flight tests.

### 热端部件技术（HOST）项目
### Hot Section Technology (HOST) Project

**English:** In the early 1980's, the life of commercial engine hot sections was considerably shorter than desired. NASA's HOST Project was conducted to focus technologies for aerothermodynamic and structural analysis of combustors and turbines toward more accurate predictions of hardware life (fig. 12). Therefore, for the first time, the aerothermodynamics to predict the thermal environment, heat transfer, and resulting thermal loads on components were combined with materials behavior to predict the structural response and resulting life. Before the HOST Project, life prediction was a primarily empirical art; if a new component differed significantly from previous experience, life prediction was inaccurate and ultimately dependent on life testing and redesign if problems arose. HOST has combined the analytical approach with the development of advanced instrumentation used in benchmark quality experiments to better characterize the actual hot-section environment and to verify computer codes. As a result, more accurate analytical predictions of hot-section life are now possible.

**中文：** 在 20 世纪 80 年代初，商用发动机热端部件（hot section）的寿命远低于期望值。NASA 开展了 HOST 项目（Hot Section Technology Project），旨在集中燃烧室和涡轮的气动热力学（aerothermodynamic）与结构分析技术，以更准确地预测硬件寿命（图 12）。因此，首次将用于预测热环境、传热及部件由此产生的热载荷的气动热力学，与材料行为相结合，以预测结构响应及由此产生的寿命。在 HOST 项目之前，寿命预测主要是一门经验性的技艺；如果某个新部件与以往经验有显著差异，寿命预测就会不准确，最终不得不依赖寿命试验，并在出现问题时进行重新设计。HOST 将分析方法与先进测量仪器（instrumentation）的开发相结合，用于基准质量实验，以更好地表征实际热端环境并验证计算机程序。因此，现在已能对热端寿命作出更准确的分析预测。

## 涡轮叶片材料的发展趋势
## Turbine Blade Materials Trends

**English:** From the beginning of the turbine engine in the 1930's, the materials have limited its efficiency and maximum thrust. In the 1950's and 1960's turbine blade materials were first wrought and later conventionally cast. During that period, use temperatures increased at a moderate pace (fig. 15). Beginning in the 1970's, new materials processing methods accelerated the pace at which use temperature increased. During this period, Lewis developed oxide-dispersion-strengthened (ODS) superalloys in-house and through contracts, and subsequently spun off the technology to industry to provide commercial alloys. The 1950's Lewis experience with tungsten-fiber-reinforced superalloys has been recently used to manufacture the first silicon-carbide-fiber/titanium-aluminide-matrix composite material and to characterize it. These materials, combined with Lewis-developed thermal barrier coatings, will provide surface use temperatures up to 2300 °F in the 1990's. The Advanced High Temperature Engine Materials Program, beginning this year, will develop materials like the silicon-carbide-fiber-reaction-bonded-silicon-nitride material identified in the Lewis in-house program for use in the 21st century at temperatures of 2500 °F and higher. These new materials, through new higher values of use temperature, provide the opportunity of reaching new heights in turbine engine efficiency and thrust at speeds from subsonic to high supersonic if the accompanying aerodynamic and structural technologies can also be developed to allow maximum use of the material capabilities.

**中文：** 自 20 世纪 30 年代涡轮发动机问世以来，材料一直限制着其效率和最大推力。在 20 世纪 50 年代和 60 年代，涡轮叶片材料最初采用锻造工艺，后来采用常规铸造。在此期间，使用温度以中等速度提高（图 15）。从 20 世纪 70 年代开始，新的材料加工方法加快了使用温度提高的步伐。在此期间，刘易斯通过自有研究和合同方式开发了氧化物弥散强化（oxide-dispersion-strengthened, ODS）高温合金（superalloy），并随后将该技术转移给工业界以提供商用合金。20 世纪 50 年代刘易斯在钨纤维增强高温合金方面的经验，近来被用于制造并表征首个碳化硅纤维/钛铝化合物基体（silicon-carbide-fiber/titanium-aluminide-matrix）复合材料。这些材料与刘易斯研制的热障涂层相结合，将在 20 世纪 90 年代提供高达 2300 °F 的表面使用温度。今年启动的"先进高温发动机材料计划"（Advanced High Temperature Engine Materials Program）将开发诸如刘易斯自有计划中确定的碳化硅纤维-反应烧结氮化硅（silicon-carbide-fiber-reaction-bonded-silicon-nitride）材料，供 21 世纪在 2500 °F 及更高温度下使用。这些新材料通过更高的使用温度值，为在从亚声速到高超声速的速度范围内将涡轮发动机的效率和推力提升到新高度提供了机会——前提是与之配套的气动和结构技术也能够得到发展，以最大限度地发挥材料的能力。

All the ceramic parts, including the turbine rotor, have been demonstrated in an engine at 2200 °F for 85 hours at 70 percent of design speed. A turbine rotor has been tested in an engine at 1950 °F and 100 percent of design speed for several hours. Future work is aimed at component reliability through improved materials, design, and manufacturing techniques to increase the overall reliability of ceramic engine components.

**中文：** 我们小发动机技术工作的另一要素涉及陶瓷、不冷却的径流式涡轮机械（turbomachinery）。多年来，刘易斯一直为能源部（Department of Energy）管理"汽车燃气涡轮计划"（Automotive Gas Turbine Program）。该计划的主要重点是推进陶瓷技术，使这些脆性材料能够被认真考虑用于高性能涡轮机械。该计划的目标是制造并演示能够在 2500 °F 的小发动机环境中工作的陶瓷部件。已经取得了显著进展。如图 20 所示，制造技术已从简单试棒和实验室试样的制造发展到发动机品质的复杂零件。图中所示的静止部件已在目标温度 2500 °F 下进行了长时间的台架试验。包括涡轮转子在内的所有陶瓷部件，已在发动机中以 70% 设计转速、2200 °F 下完成了 85 小时的演示。一台涡轮转子已在发动机中以 100% 设计转速、1950 °F 下试验了数小时。未来工作的目标是通过改进材料、设计和制造工艺来提高部件可靠性，从而提高陶瓷发动机部件的总体可靠性。

## 远程高速飞行
## Long-Range, High-Speed Flight

**English:** While NASA Lewis will continue to work on subsonic propulsion technology, the major part of its aeropropulsion program is shifting toward propulsion systems for long-range supersonic and hypersonic aircraft (fig. 21). The bottom picture represents configurations being studied for second-generation commercial supersonic transports that will probably be limited to turbine engine propulsion and hydrocarbon (JP-type) fuels. **中文：** NASA 正在研究的大多数高速飞行推进概念如图 22 所示。其中许多在主推进流路中采用涡轮发动机硬件，但也有一些非涡轮机械系统正在为特殊应用而研究，例如加速任务（如空气液化循环（air liquefaction cycle）和超燃冲压循环（scramjet cycle））。高速巡航任务通常使用涡轮发动机进行加速和巡航，除非巡航温度对发动机而言过高。在这些极高速情况下，则在双循环（dual cycle）中采用冲压（ramjet）或超燃冲压（scramjet）推进。对于商用运输机的超声速巡航，NASA 在与工业界的联合计划中正在研究多种涡轮发动机循环，其中包括采用超声速通流风扇（supersonic-throughflow fan）的涡扇发动机，这似乎是一个很有前景的新概念。

### 高速民用运输机
### High-Speed Civil Transport

**English:** Returning to the efficiency figure (fig. 2), a supersonic cruise aircraft goal has been added (fig. 23). Because of the large ram-pressure-ratio in supersonic flight, the Concorde propulsion system achieves a relatively high overall efficiency in spite of its 1960's technology. The goal of the current NASA program is to increase that efficiency to at least 60 percent through the use of advanced materials, structures, aerodynamics, and cycles like the one using a supersonic-throughflow fan.

### 超声速通流风扇技术
### Supersonic Throughflow Fan Technology

**English:** The advantages of the supersonic fan relative to a baseline afterburning turbofan are illustrated in figure 24. The simpler inlet and fan are lighter weight and more efficient by avoiding the complexity of slowing the external flow to subsonic speeds before introducing it to the fan. These advantages provide about a 10-percent decrease in specific fuel consumption and about a 25-percent reduction in propulsion weight, which leads to a 22-percent increase in aircraft range. Since the feasibility of maintaining supersonic flow through a turbomachinery stage has never been demonstrated, Lewis has initiated an exploratory program to investigate the feasibility of the supersonic fan component.

**中文：** 图 24 展示了超声速风扇相对于基准加力涡扇发动机（afterburning turbofan）的优势。更简单的进气道和风扇通过避免将外部气流减速到亚声速后再引入风扇的复杂过程，从而重量更轻、效率更高。这些优势使耗油率降低约 10%，推进系统重量减少约 25%，从而使飞机航程增加 22%。由于维持气流以超声速通过涡轮机械级的可行性从未得到验证，刘易斯已启动一项探索性计划，以研究超声速风扇部件的可行性。

**中文：** *图 24* — 超声速通流涡扇对一架 300 座、3 马赫、航程 5500 海里（nmi）商用运输机的收益（在安装效率、推进系统重量、飞机省油等方面对比超声速风扇与非加力涡扇基准）。

### 极高速推进
### Very High Speed Propulsion

**English:** As we direct attention to higher speed regimes, hybrid propulsion systems beyond turbomachinery cycles must be considered. Figure 26 presents the specific impulse of the basic airbreathing propulsion cycles and compares them with the best rockets. The cycles with turbomachinery provide the highest specific impulse at speeds up to about Mach 5 where it becomes too hot for the turbomachinery to produce enough pressure ratio to overcome the inefficiency of its components. The subsonic ramjet then provides the highest specific impulse until about Mach 10 where molecular dissociation reduces its impulse below that of the scramjet. Airbreathing cycles always have a higher impulse than rockets but are much more difficult to operate at the higher Mach numbers. Work at Lewis and other NASA centers is aimed at extending the use of airbreathing cycles to Mach numbers higher than the Mach 3+ flown by the YF12.

**中文：** 当我们将注意力转向更高速度范围时，必须考虑超越涡轮机械循环的混合推进系统。图 26 给出了基本吸气式（airbreathing）推进循环的比冲（specific impulse），并将其与最好的火箭进行了比较。带涡轮机械的循环在速度高达约 5 马赫时提供最高的比冲，此时温度过高，涡轮机械无法产生足够的压比来克服其部件的低效。随后，亚声速燃烧冲压发动机在直到约 10 马赫之前提供最高的比冲，超过该速度后分子离解（molecular dissociation）会使其比冲降到低于超燃冲压发动机的水平。吸气式循环的比冲始终高于火箭，但在更高马赫数下要难操作得多。刘易斯及其他 NASA 中心的工作旨在将吸气式循环的应用扩展到高于 YF12 所飞行的 3+ 马赫的马赫数。

**English:** *Figure 26.* — Performance comparison of high-speed airbreathing and rocket propulsion.

**中文：** *图 26* — 高速吸气式推进与火箭推进的性能比较（纵轴为比冲（秒），横轴为马赫数；对比涡喷、亚声速燃烧冲压、超声速燃烧冲压（超燃冲压）和火箭）。

**English:** A joint study of a military Mach 5 cruise aircraft by Lewis and Langley Research Centers and industry partners identified an over/under turboramjet cycle to provide desirable acceleration and cruise performance. The Mach 5 inlet illustrated in figure 27 represents the ramjet configuration with the turbine engine compartment off for high-speed cruise. The experimental inlet hardware was recently delivered to Lewis for test in the 10- by 10-Foot Supersonic Wind Tunnel. Results of a fully viscous three-dimensional analysis displayed in the figure indicate that the sidewall boundary layer will collect on the cowl side of the inlet sidewall and cause separation, which would probably cause inlet unstart. Analytical results such as these were used to design a bleed system for the experimental hardware that will be tested in the near future.

**中文：** 刘易斯和兰利研究中心以及工业合作伙伴对一架军用 5 马赫巡航飞机进行的联合研究，确定了一种上/下置涡轮冲压（over/under turboramjet）循环，以提供理想的加速和巡航性能。图 27 所示的 5 马赫进气道代表了在高速巡航时关闭涡轮发动机舱的冲压发动机构型。该实验进气道硬件最近已交付刘易斯，用于在 10×10 英尺超声速风洞中进行试验。图中显示的全粘性三维分析结果表明，侧壁边界层将在进气道侧壁的整流罩（cowl）一侧聚集并引起分离，这很可能导致进气道不起动（unstart）。诸如此类的分析结果被用于为实验硬件设计一套抽吸（bleed）系统，该硬件将在不久的将来进行试验。

**English:** While supersonic combustion ramjets (scramjets) were envisioned over 20 years ago, no one has yet proven their practical use. ## 结束语
## Concluding Remarks

**English:** The U.S. aeropropulsion industry has been very successful in competing in the world market for powering modern aircraft. NASA takes great pride in its contributions to that success, some of which have been reviewed in this paper. As the world competition grows, it will become harder to maintain our current leadership. NASA's current aeropropulsion program will continue to support that leadership by emphasizing technology that will provide future opportunities for major advances in propulsion efficiency and durability. While we have reviewed the highlights of that program, we could not cover it in sufficient detail nor describe enough of its programs to gain a full appreciation of its breadth and scope. Also, we have primarily concentrated on propulsion technology for commercial aircraft applications; however, much of NASA's propulsion technology advances are also applicable to military aircraft systems. The papers that follow will provide a broader description of the NASA aeropropulsion program, which should help lead the industry into continuing to produce the best propulsion systems for commercial and military aircraft into the 21st century.

**中文：** 美国航空推进工业在为现代飞机提供动力的世界市场竞争中非常成功。NASA 为这一成功所作的贡献深感自豪，本文已回顾了其中的一部分。随着世界竞争的加剧，保持我们当前的领先地位将变得更加困难。NASA 当前的航空推进计划将通过强调能够为推进效率和耐久性带来重大进步提供未来机遇的技术，继续支撑这一领先地位。尽管我们回顾了该计划的亮点，但无法以足够的细节加以介绍，也无法描述其足够多的子计划，以使人全面领会其广度和范围。此外，我们主要集中于面向商用飞机应用的推进技术；然而，NASA 的许多推进技术进步同样适用于军用飞机系统。后续的论文将对 NASA 航空推进计划作更广泛的描述，这应有助于引领该工业界在迈入 21 世纪的进程中继续制造出面向商用和军用飞机的最佳推进系统。
