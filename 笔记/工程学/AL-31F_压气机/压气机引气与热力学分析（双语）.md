# Development of flow and efficiency characteristics of an axial compressor with an analytical method including cooling air extraction and variable inlet guide vane angle

## 摘要 Abstract

**English:** The development of a reliable mathematical model of an axial compressor requires applying flow and efficiency characteristics. This approach provides performance parameters of a machine depending on varying conditions. In this paper, a method for developing characteristics of an axial compressor is presented, based on general compressor maps available in the literature or measurement data from industrial facilities. The novelty that constitutes the core of this article is introducing an improved method describing the performance lines of an axial compressor with the modified ellipse equation. The proposed model is extended with bleed air extraction for the purposes of cooling the blades in the expander part of the gas turbine. The variable inlet guide vanes angle is also considered using the vane angle correction factor. All developed dependencies are fully analytical. The presented approach does not require knowledge of machine geometry. The set of input parameters is based on reference data. The presented approach makes it possible to determine the allowed operating area and study the machine's performance in variable conditions. The introduced mathematical correlations provide a fully analytical study of optimum operating points concerning the chosen criterion. The final section presents a mathematical model of an axial compressor built using the developed method. A detailed study of the exemplary flow and efficiency characteristics of an axial compressor operating with a gas turbine is also provided.

**中文：** 建立可靠的轴流压气机数学模型需要应用流量特性与效率特性。这一方法能够给出机器在不同工况下的性能参数。本文提出了一种基于文献中通用压气机特性图（compressor maps）或工业设施实测数据来建立轴流压气机特性的方法。本文的核心创新在于引入一种改进方法，用修正的椭圆方程（modified ellipse equation）来描述轴流压气机的性能线。所提出的模型还扩展了引气抽取（bleed air extraction），用于冷却燃气轮机膨胀段的叶片。同时，利用叶片角修正系数（vane angle correction factor）来考虑可变进口导叶（inlet guide vanes）角度的影响。所建立的全部关系式均为完全解析的。所提出的方法不需要知道机器的几何结构，其输入参数集基于参考数据。本方法能够确定允许工作区域，并研究机器在变工况下的性能。所引入的数学关联式针对所选准则提供了对最优工作点的完全解析研究。文章最后一节给出了采用所建立方法构建的轴流压气机数学模型，并对与燃气轮机协同工作的轴流压气机的示例流量特性和效率特性进行了详细研究。

## 1. 引言
## 1. Introduction

**English:** The axial compressor is one of the main components included in a gas turbine unit. A reliable modelling and accurate representation of the performance of an axial compressor is possible using individual flow and efficiency characteristics. However, this data is hardly available because manufacturers do not provide detailed specification defining the operation of the machine to public knowledge. Furthermore, the data provided by manufacturers may not be applicable for a unit that has been operating for a considerable time and its performance has deteriorated. Commercial engineering software does not have fully developed tools in this area either. One of the alternatives to this state of matter is the usage and adaptation of general maps available in the literature [1, 2].

**中文：** 轴流压气机是燃气轮机机组的主要部件之一。利用各台机器独有的流量特性与效率特性，可以对轴流压气机的性能进行可靠的建模和准确的表征。然而，这类数据很难获得，因为制造商并不向公众提供定义机器运行的详细规范。此外，制造商提供的数据可能并不适用于已运行相当长时间、性能已经退化的机组。商用工程软件在该领域也没有完善的工具。应对这一现状的替代方案之一，是使用并改造文献中可获得的通用特性图 [1, 2]。

---

**English:** The technological progress in the development of gas turbine systems resulted in axial compressors being highly advanced units. This provides a challenge in designing reliable and precise mathematical models. Increasing the flexibility of gas turbine unit performance and improving its efficiency across a more comprehensive load range became possible by introducing movable blades of the first or several first nozzle rows [3, 4]. In turn, the aim to maximise the power and efficiency of the system is achieved, among other possibilities, by increasing the inlet temperature to the expansion section of the gas turbine unit [5]. However, this requires advanced cooling systems using bleed air from the compressor part [2, 6]. The discussed aspects should be considered in the development of the mathematical model of the system and the use of general maps of axial compressors.

**中文：** 燃气轮机系统发展中的技术进步使得轴流压气机成为高度先进的部件，这给建立可靠而精确的数学模型带来了挑战。通过引入第一级或前几级喷嘴排的可动叶片 [3, 4]，使得提高燃气轮机机组性能的灵活性、并在更宽广的负荷范围内改善其效率成为可能。进而，为了实现系统功率与效率的最大化，途径之一是提高燃气轮机机组膨胀段的进口温度 [5]。然而，这需要采用从压气机部分引气的先进冷却系统 [2, 6]。在建立系统数学模型和使用轴流压气机通用特性图时，应当考虑上述各方面。

---

**English:** This paper presents a method of mathematical description and implementation of general maps of an axial compressor in a mathematical model of a gas turbine system. The lines of operation are characterised by the equation of an ellipse. Attempts to use a similar approach can occasionally be found in the subject literature [7, 8]. The novelty of the presented approach is the possibility of arbitrary displacement and rotation of ellipses describing the operating lines and dependence of characteristic values of the ellipse upon reduced parameters defining the operation of the axial compressor. Additionally, the proposed model is extended by including bleed air intake for blade cooling in the expansion section of the gas turbine unit. Moreover, the effect of variable inlet guide vane (IGV) angle is also considered using the vane angle correction factor (VACF). All the developed dependencies are fully analytical. This approach allows visualising the relationships between the axial compressor performance parameters and fully understanding their influence on the machine operation. Obtaining such an interpretation can be difficult or even impossible using empirical functions.

**中文：** 本文提出了一种在燃气轮机系统数学模型中对轴流压气机通用特性图进行数学描述与实现的方法。工作线用椭圆方程来表征。在本主题的文献中偶尔可以发现采用类似方法的尝试 [7, 8]。本文方法的创新之处在于：可以对描述工作线的椭圆进行任意平移和旋转，并使椭圆的特征值依赖于定义轴流压气机运行的折合参数。此外，所提出的模型扩展了对燃气轮机机组膨胀段叶片冷却引气抽取的考虑。而且，利用叶片角修正系数（VACF）也考虑了可变进口导叶（IGV）角的影响。所建立的全部关系式均为完全解析的。该方法能够可视化轴流压气机各性能参数之间的关系，并充分理解它们对机器运行的影响。若使用经验函数，要获得这样的解释会很困难甚至不可能。

---

**English:** In the final part of the article, exemplary calculations for an axial compressor model that is a part of a gas turbine unit are presented. The created computer programme is a reflection of the developed mathematical relationships. The axial compressor model cooperates with the combustion chamber and the expansion part of the gas turbine unit. The generated results allow investigating the influence of various factors (mainly ambient temperature and the degree of opening of the first nozzle row) on the machine performance. In particular, the achieved pressure ratio, the axial compressor's internal efficiency and the total fraction of the air cooling the blades in the expansion part of the turbine set are analysed. The conducted study enables identification of the points of optimum machine operation depending on external conditions.

**中文：** 在文章的最后部分，给出了作为燃气轮机机组组成部分的轴流压气机模型的示例计算。所编写的计算机程序是对所建立数学关系式的体现。该轴流压气机模型与燃气轮机机组的燃烧室和膨胀段协同工作。所生成的结果可以研究各种因素（主要是环境温度和第一级喷嘴排的开度）对机器性能的影响。特别地，分析了所达到的压比、轴流压气机的内效率以及冷却涡轮膨胀段叶片的空气总份额。所进行的研究能够根据外部条件识别机器的最优运行点。

## 2. 轴流压气机通用特性图
## 2. General map of an axial compressor

**English:** The general maps of axial compressors are widely available in [4, 9–11]. They are developed based on dimensionless analysis, which uses reduced parameters. It allows comparing the performance of machines with similar geometry and analysing the performance of a particular compressor under different than nominal conditions. Using reduced parameters implies the reference of the current operating conditions of the machine to nominal values [2]. The essential reduced parameters include:

**中文：** 轴流压气机的通用特性图在文献 [4, 9–11] 中广泛可得。它们是基于无量纲分析建立的，该分析使用折合参数（reduced parameters）。这使得能够比较几何形状相似的机器的性能，并分析某台特定压气机在不同于额定工况下的性能。使用折合参数意味着将机器当前的运行条件折算到额定值 [2]。主要的折合参数包括：

---

**English:** • reduced pressure ratio
$$\pi_{\text{red}} = \frac{\pi}{\pi_{\text{nom}}}, \tag{1}$$

• reduced rotational speed
$$n_{\text{red}} = \frac{n}{n_{\text{nom}}} \sqrt{\frac{(T_{\text{in}})_{\text{nom}}}{T_{\text{in}}}}, \tag{2}$$

• reduced mass flow
$$\varphi_{\text{red}} = \frac{\dot{m}}{\dot{m}_{\text{nom}}} \frac{(p_{\text{in}})_{\text{nom}}}{p_{\text{in}}} \sqrt{\frac{T_{\text{in}}}{(T_{\text{in}})_{\text{nom}}}}, \tag{3}$$

**中文：** • 折合压比
$$\pi_{\text{red}} = \frac{\pi}{\pi_{\text{nom}}}, \tag{1}$$

• 折合转速
$$n_{\text{red}} = \frac{n}{n_{\text{nom}}} \sqrt{\frac{(T_{\text{in}})_{\text{nom}}}{T_{\text{in}}}}, \tag{2}$$

• 折合质量流量
$$\varphi_{\text{red}} = \frac{\dot{m}}{\dot{m}_{\text{nom}}} \frac{(p_{\text{in}})_{\text{nom}}}{p_{\text{in}}} \sqrt{\frac{T_{\text{in}}}{(T_{\text{in}})_{\text{nom}}}}, \tag{3}$$

---

**English:** • reduced internal efficiency
$$\eta_{\text{red}} = \frac{\eta}{\eta_{\text{nom}}}. \tag{4}$$

**中文：** • 折合内效率
$$\eta_{\text{red}} = \frac{\eta}{\eta_{\text{nom}}}. \tag{4}$$

---

**English:** The general map of the axial compressor is usually given in reduced pressure ratio, $\pi_{\text{red}}$, and reduced inlet mass flow, $\varphi_{\text{red}}$. Typically it includes two sets of data. The first dataset contains the lines of constant reduced rotational speed, $n_{\text{red}}$, it is a flow characteristic. The second dataset regards the lines of constant reduced internal efficiency, $\eta_{\text{red}}$, it is an efficiency characteristic. These characteristics are commonly presented on a unified plot. Unambiguous determination of the operating point of an axial compressor is possible, assuming the knowledge of ambient conditions, values of nominal machine parameters and inlet airflow [2].

**中文：** 轴流压气机的通用特性图通常以折合压比 $\pi_{\text{red}}$ 和折合进口质量流量 $\varphi_{\text{red}}$ 给出。它通常包含两组数据。第一组数据包含等折合转速 $n_{\text{red}}$ 线，即流量特性。第二组数据涉及等折合内效率 $\eta_{\text{red}}$ 线，即效率特性。这些特性通常绘制在同一张图上。在已知环境条件、机器额定参数值和进口空气流量的前提下，可以唯一地确定轴流压气机的工作点 [2]。

---

**English:** Determining the performance of an axial compressor using the general maps should start with calculating the reduced values of mass flow, $\varphi_{\text{red}}$, and the rotational speed, $n_{\text{red}}$. Then, using the flow characteristic, the point on the constant line of reduced rotational speed, $n_{\text{red}}$, that corresponds to the value of the reduced mass flow, $\varphi_{\text{red}}$, is found – it indicates the value of the reduced pressure ratio, $\pi_{\text{red}}$. In the last step, the efficiency characteristic should be used, on which it is necessary to find the line of reduced internal efficiency, $\eta_{\text{red}}$, crossing the point determined by the values of the reduced parameters of mass flow, $\varphi_{\text{red}}$, and pressure ratio, $\pi_{\text{red}}$.

**中文：** 使用通用特性图确定轴流压气机性能时，应从计算质量流量的折合值 $\varphi_{\text{red}}$ 和转速的折合值 $n_{\text{red}}$ 开始。然后，利用流量特性，在等折合转速 $n_{\text{red}}$ 线上找到与折合质量流量 $\varphi_{\text{red}}$ 值相对应的点——它给出折合压比 $\pi_{\text{red}}$ 的值。最后一步，应使用效率特性，在其上找到穿过由折合质量流量 $\varphi_{\text{red}}$ 和折合压比 $\pi_{\text{red}}$ 值所确定的点的折合内效率 $\eta_{\text{red}}$ 线。

---

**English:** The allowable operating area of an axial compressor is limited on one side by the surge line. It indicates the maximum outlet pressure above which pumping occurs in operation. This phenomenon is hazardous and undesirable during machine performance. In practice, during load operation of a compressor, the lower limit is mostly the maximum volumetric flow of the medium resulting from the geometry of the flow system.

**中文：** 轴流压气机的允许工作区域一侧受喘振线（surge line）限制。它指出了最大出口压力，超过该压力时运行中会发生喘振（pumping）。这种现象是危险的，且在机器运行中是不希望出现的。在实际中，压气机带负荷运行时，下限大多是由流道几何形状决定的介质最大体积流量。

---

**English:** The lines of constant reduced rotational speed, $n_{\text{red}}$, and constant reduced internal efficiency, $\eta_{\text{red}}$, resemble an ellipse. Attempts of mathematical description of the indicated characteristics using the ellipse equation can be found in the literature [7, 8]. However, most of them are founded on the basic ellipse equation with constant parameters and central point set in the origin of the coordinate system. In the following part of this paper, the equation of an ellipse moved freely about the centre of the coordinate system and rotated by an arbitrary angle is discussed.

**中文：** 等折合转速 $n_{\text{red}}$ 线和等折合内效率 $\eta_{\text{red}}$ 线形似椭圆。在文献 [7, 8] 中可以找到用椭圆方程对所述特性进行数学描述的尝试。然而，它们大多基于参数恒定、中心位于坐标系原点的基本椭圆方程。在本文以下部分，讨论了能够绕坐标系中心自由平移、并以任意角度旋转的椭圆方程。

---

**中文：** 为实现所建立的模型，选取了文献 [2] 中给出的通用特性图。所给出的数据涉及动力压气机的一组级。该图被数字化。详细数据见图 1 及本文后续部分。

## 3. 椭圆方程
## 3. Equation of an ellipse

**English:** Consideration of selecting a suitable model equation began with an analysis of the fundamental equation of an ellipse with $A$ and $B$ semi-axes along the axis of the coordinate system and central point at the origin of the coordinate system [12]
$$\frac{x^2}{A^2} + \frac{y^2}{B^2} = 1. \tag{5}$$

**中文：** 选择合适模型方程的考量始于对基本椭圆方程的分析，该椭圆的 $A$、$B$ 半轴沿坐标系坐标轴方向、中心点位于坐标系原点 [12]
$$\frac{x^2}{A^2} + \frac{y^2}{B^2} = 1. \tag{5}$$

---

**English:** In the subsequent step, the possibility of free displacement of the ellipse regarding the origin of the coordinate system by the vector $v$ was added [12]
$$v = [x_0, y_0]. \tag{6}$$

**中文：** 在下一步中，加入了椭圆相对于坐标系原点按矢量 $v$ 自由平移的可能性 [12]
$$v = [x_0, y_0]. \tag{6}$$

---

**English:** The study of the displacement vector led to determining the relationship between the original and displaced coordinate systems
$$\begin{bmatrix} x_v \\ y_v \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} x \\ y \end{bmatrix} + \begin{bmatrix} x_0 \\ y_0 \end{bmatrix}. \tag{7}$$

**中文：** 对位移矢量的研究得出了原坐标系与平移坐标系之间的关系
$$\begin{bmatrix} x_v \\ y_v \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} x \\ y \end{bmatrix} + \begin{bmatrix} x_0 \\ y_0 \end{bmatrix}. \tag{7}$$

---

**English:** From the matrix notation (7), it was possible to identify the correlations for the individual coordinates in the new coordinate system:
$$x_v = x + x_0, \tag{8}$$
$$y_v = y + y_0. \tag{9}$$

**中文：** 由矩阵表示式 (7)，可以确定新坐标系中各坐标的关系式：
$$x_v = x + x_0, \tag{8}$$
$$y_v = y + y_0. \tag{9}$$

---

**English:** The final step was to add the possibility of rotating the ellipse by an arbitrary angle $\alpha$ in the clockwise direction. The inclusion of rotation also required defining the relationship between the original and rotated coordinate systems. The most convenient way was the matrix notation [12]
$$\begin{bmatrix} x_{\text{rot}} \\ y_{\text{rot}} \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \cos\alpha & \sin\alpha \\ -\sin\alpha & \cos\alpha \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} x \\ y \end{bmatrix}. \tag{10}$$

**中文：** 最后一步是加入将椭圆沿顺时针方向旋转任意角度 $\alpha$ 的可能性。包含旋转还需要定义原坐标系与旋转坐标系之间的关系。最方便的方式是矩阵表示式 [12]
$$\begin{bmatrix} x_{\text{rot}} \\ y_{\text{rot}} \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \cos\alpha & \sin\alpha \\ -\sin\alpha & \cos\alpha \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} x \\ y \end{bmatrix}. \tag{10}$$

---

**English:** Deriving the particular relations for the coordinates in the rotated coordinate system resulted in the following dependencies:
$$x_{\text{rot}} = x \cos\alpha + y \sin\alpha, \tag{11}$$
$$y_{\text{rot}} = y \cos\alpha - x \sin\alpha. \tag{12}$$

**中文：** 推导旋转坐标系中各坐标的具体关系式，得到如下关系：

$$y_{\text{rot}} = y \cos\alpha - x \sin\alpha. \tag{12}$$

**English:** Due to the linear nature of the transformations associated with displacement and rotation, the order in which they were applied to the ellipse equation was irrelevant. However, it should be noted that if the rotation of the ellipse is applied first, the displacement vector $v$ also has to be rotated. Considering relations (7) to (12) in Eq. $$\frac{(x \cos\alpha - y \sin\alpha - x_0)^2}{A^2} + \frac{(x \sin\alpha + y \cos\alpha - y_0)^2}{B^2} = 1. \tag{13}$$
was used to describe the lines of constant reduced rotational speed, $n_{\text{red}}$, and constant reduced internal efficiency, $\eta_{\text{red}}$. The algorithm detailed in [13] was used to obtain the constants present in the ellipse equation (13). The method allows the best fitting of the ellipse to a set of given points using the least-squares method. The calculation of the characteristic values is performed directly analytically without the iterative process. The method is marked by simplicity, stability and ease of implementation in the form of computational code.

**中文：** 由于平移和旋转所涉及的变换具有线性性质，它们应用于椭圆方程的顺序无关紧要。但应注意，如果先对椭圆进行旋转，则位移矢量 $v$ 也必须随之旋转。将关系式 (7) 至 (12) 代入式 (5)，得到了平移并旋转后椭圆的通用方程。关系式
$$\frac{(x \cos\alpha - y \sin\alpha - x_0)^2}{A^2} + \frac{(x \sin\alpha + y \cos\alpha - y_0)^2}{B^2} = 1. \tag{13}$$
被用于描述等折合转速 $n_{\text{red}}$ 线和等折合内效率 $\eta_{\text{red}}$ 线。采用文献 [13] 中详述的算法来获得椭圆方程 (13) 中的常数。该方法利用最小二乘法将椭圆最佳拟合到一组给定点。特征值的计算是直接解析进行的，无需迭代过程。该方法以简单、稳定、易于以计算代码形式实现而著称。

## 5. 等折合转速线

For the analysis, the lines of constant reduced rotational speed $n_{\text{red}}$ corresponding to the values: 0.8, 0.9, 1.0, 1.1, and 1.2 were selected. ## 7. 可变进口导叶角的影响
## 7. Impact of variable inlet guide vanes angle

**English:** Movable blades of the first stage of the compressor provide increased flexibility in the operation of the gas turbine system and improve the unit's efficiency over a wide performance range. The variable angle of the IGVs changes the geometry of the machine flow system. Considering this when analysing unit performance should be reflected in the development and adjustment of general compressor characteristics. The inclusion of the variable IGVs angle in the equations describing the relationships between the reduced parameters of pressure ratio, $\pi_{\text{red}}$, and mass flow, $\varphi_{\text{red}}$, was possible through the use of the parameter VACF, which expresses the percentage change in the mass flow of the inlet air with respect to the change in the position of the first stage guide vanes
$$\text{VACF} = \frac{\dot{m}_{\text{max}} - \dot{m}_{\text{min}}}{\dot{m}_{\text{max}} (\text{IGV}_{\text{max}} - \text{IGV}_{\text{min}})}. \tag{19}$$

**中文：** 压气机第一级的可动叶片提高了燃气轮机系统运行的灵活性，并在宽广的性能范围内改善了机组的效率。IGV 的可变角度改变了机器流道的几何形状。在分析机组性能时考虑这一点，应当体现在通用压气机特性的建立与调整中。通过使用参数 VACF，可以在描述压比折合参数 $\pi_{\text{red}}$ 与质量流量折合参数 $\varphi_{\text{red}}$ 之间关系的方程中纳入可变 IGV 角度，该参数表示进口空气质量流量相对于第一级导叶位置变化的百分比变化
$$\text{VACF} = \frac{\dot{m}_{\text{max}} - \dot{m}_{\text{min}}}{\dot{m}_{\text{max}} (\text{IGV}_{\text{max}} - \text{IGV}_{\text{min}})}. \tag{19}$$

---

**English:** For power compressors, the value of the VACF parameter oscillates around 1% [1].

**中文：** 对于动力压气机，VACF 参数的值在 1% 左右波动 [1]。

---

**English:** According to [1], changing the IGVs angle affects only the flow characteristics of the axial compressor. The inclusion of the parameter does not modify the shape of the lines of constant reduced rotational speed, $n_{\text{red}}$; it only leads to a displacement of the characteristics along the horizontal axis of the coordinate system.

**中文：** 根据文献 [1]，改变 IGV 角度仅影响轴流压气机的流量特性。纳入该参数不会改变等折合转速 $n_{\text{red}}$ 线的形状；它只会导致特性沿坐标系水平轴平移。

---

**English:** The actual mass flow rate of the compressor's inlet air with the VACF parameter can be written in the general form
$$\dot{m} = \dot{m}_{\text{max}} [1 - \text{VACF} (\text{IGV}_{\text{max}} - \text{IGV})]. \tag{20}$$

**中文：** 含 VACF 参数的压气机进口空气实际质量流量可写成如下通用形式
$$\dot{m} = \dot{m}_{\text{max}} [1 - \text{VACF} (\text{IGV}_{\text{max}} - \text{IGV})]. \tag{20}$$

---

**English:** The difference between the maximum mass flow rate of the inlet air entering the compressor and the current value can be written in the general form
$$\frac{\Delta \dot{m}_{\text{IGV}}}{\dot{m}_{\text{max}}} = \text{VACF} (\text{IGV}_{\text{max}} - \text{IGV}). \tag{21}$$

**中文：** 进入压气机的进口空气最大质量流量与当前值之间的差可写成如下通用形式
$$\frac{\Delta \dot{m}_{\text{IGV}}}{\dot{m}_{\text{max}}} = \text{VACF} (\text{IGV}_{\text{max}} - \text{IGV}). \tag{21}$$

---

**English:** Relating this difference to the nominal values of the machine resulted in the expression for the reduced mass flow loss (22) due to flow throttling at the compressor inlet
$$\Delta \varphi_{\text{IGV}} = \text{VACF} (\text{IGV}_{\text{max}} - \text{IGV}) \frac{(p_{\text{in}})_{\text{nom}}}{p_{\text{in}}} \sqrt{\frac{T_{\text{in}}}{(T_{\text{in}})_{\text{nom}}}}. \tag{22}$$

**中文：** 将此差值与机器的额定值相关联，得到了由压气机进口处流动节流引起的折合质量流量损失表达式 (22)
$$\Delta \varphi_{\text{IGV}} = \text{VACF} (\text{IGV}_{\text{max}} - \text{IGV}) \frac{(p_{\text{in}})_{\text{nom}}}{p_{\text{in}}} \sqrt{\frac{T_{\text{in}}}{(T_{\text{in}})_{\text{nom}}}}. \tag{22}$$

---

**English:** The value of the parameter $\Delta\varphi_{IGV}$ specifies the displacement of the compressor flow characteristics along the plot's horizontal axis. The transformation is linear and can be included in model Eq. (16), similarly to the displacement by a vector (6). An example of the effect of changing the IGVs angle on the obtained general characteristics is presented in Fig. 11.

**中文：** 参数 $\Delta\varphi_{IGV}$ 的值规定了压气机流量特性沿图中水平轴的平移量。该变换是线性的，可以纳入模型方程 (16)，方式与按矢量 (6) 平移类似。改变 IGV 角度对所得通用特性影响的一个示例见图 11。

## 8. 引气抽取的影响
## 8. Impact of bleed air intake

**English:** A systematic attempt to maximise the efficiency of gas turbine units is realised, among other possibilities, by raising the inlet temperature to the expansion section. The first stages of the turbine operate in the presence of a working medium with a temperature significantly exceeding the material durability of possible metal alloys, even with the application of an additional thermal barrier coating (TBC) [4, 6]. It necessitates the introduction of additional technological solutions in the form of advanced cooling systems for the blade rows with air extracted from the compressor bleeds [2]. Consideration of the cooling air intake should be included in the analysis of the unit's performance.

**中文：** 使燃气轮机机组效率最大化的系统性尝试，途径之一是提高膨胀段的进口温度。涡轮的前几级在工质温度显著超过可能的金属合金材料耐久性的条件下运行，即便施加了附加的热障涂层（TBC）也是如此 [4, 6]。这就需要引入附加的技术解决方案，即采用从压气机引气口抽取的空气对叶片排进行先进冷却的系统 [2]。在机组性能分析中应当考虑冷却空气的抽取。

---

**English:** The correlation between the gas turbine inlet temperature and the total demand for cooling air extracted from the compressor bleeds related to the inlet mass flow is approximately a temperature linear function [15]. Interpolation of the data available in the literature for an inlet temperature of 1500°C provided an estimated value of the fraction of cooling air at the level of 20.9% – Fig. 12.

**中文：** 燃气轮机进口温度与从压气机引气口抽取、相对于进口质量流量的冷却空气总需求之间的关系，近似为温度的线性函数 [15]。对文献中可得数据进行插值，对应于 1500°C 的进口温度，给出了冷却空气份额的估计值约为 20.9%——图 12。

---

**English:** It was assumed that the presence of compressor bleeds does not affect the shape of the general flow and efficiency characteristics. The increase in the compressor inlet flow due to additional cooling intake moved the current operating point of the machine on the characteristics following the change in the reduced mass flow rate, $\varphi_{\text{red}}$. Determination of the amount of cooling air was based on the detailed model of the air film cooling process presented in [6]. Only the essential dependencies enabling calculation of the mass flow rate of the bleed air necessary to ensure the required cooling of the rows in the expansion section were presented.

**中文：** 假定压气机引气的存在不影响通用流量特性和效率特性的形状。由于附加冷却抽气导致的压气机进口流量增大，随折合质量流量 $\varphi_{\text{red}}$ 的变化使机器在特性图上的当前工作点发生移动。冷却空气量的确定基于文献 [6] 中给出的气膜冷却（air film cooling）过程的详细模型。此处仅给出确保膨胀段叶片排所需冷却所必需的引气质量流量计算所需的基本关系式。

$$\varepsilon_{\text{total}} = \frac{T_{\text{exh}} - T_{\text{blade}}}{T_{\text{exh}} - T_{\text{air}}}. \tag{23}$$

**中文：** 引入一个表征与工质流动及叶片系统几何形状相关参数的附加常数 $k_{\text{cool}} = 0.03$ [6] 以及安全系数 $\text{SF}_{\text{cool}} = 1.5$，则表示冷却空气质量流量相对于涡轮级进口处排气总质量流量份额的方程可写为
$$\frac{\dot{m}_{\text{cool}}}{\dot{m}_{\text{exh}}} = \text{SF}_{\text{cool}} k_{\text{cool}} \theta. \tag{25}$$

## 9. 轴流压气机数学模型
## 9. Mathematical model of an axial compressor

**English:** Based on the formulated dependencies, a computational programme simulating the operation of an axial compressor was developed. The mathematical model of the working medium was implemented based on [16] using the Redlich-Kwong real gas equation of state. Calculations for the compressor flow system were carried out using the stage-stacking method. The model considered the general flow and efficiency characteristics, the possibility of changing the IGVs angle and the air intake from the compressor bleeds for cooling the rows of the expansion section of the gas turbine.

**中文：** 基于所建立的各关系式，开发了一个模拟轴流压气机运行的计算程序。工质的数学模型基于文献 [16] 实现，使用 Redlich-Kwong 实际气体状态方程。压气机流道的计算采用逐级叠加法（stage-stacking method）进行。该模型考虑了通用流量特性和效率特性、改变 IGV 角度的可能性，以及从压气机引气口抽取空气以冷却燃气轮机膨胀段叶片排的情况。

---

**English:** The modelled axial compressor unit cooperated with the combustion chamber and expander. The flow system of the axial compressor had 17 stages. The nominal atmospheric air parameters at the machine inlet corresponded to normal conditions. Adjusting the flow rate at the inlet to the system was possible due to the variable IGVs angle of the first nozzle row ranging from −20° to 15°. The combustion chamber ensured a constant exhaust gas temperature of 1500°C at the expander inlet. The expansion section of the turbine consisted of four stages, the first three of which were cooled by air from the compressor system. The fourth stage did not require additional cooling. The allowable temperature of the expander blades was $T_{\text{blade}} = 770$°C. The first compressor bleed was located after the 10th stage and supplied air to the third expander nozzle row, the second bleed was located after the 14th stage and supplied air to the second nozzle row. Furthermore, part of the air from the compressor outlet was taken for cooling purposes: the first nozzle and rotor rows, the second and third rotor rows. It should be pointed out that the conducted analysis concerned only the operation of the axial compressor model. It did not include the variable parameters of operation of the combustion chamber and the expansion part of the gas turbine unit. The allowable blade temperature in the expansion section and the exhaust gas temperatures at the inlet to the specific turbine stages, which were only used to determine the bleed air flows for cooling purposes, were assumed to be constant and unchanged with the gas turbine unit load. ## 11. 总结与结论
## 11. Summary and conclusions

**English:** This paper presented a method for the mathematical description of the general flow and efficiency characteristics of an axial compressor using a modified ellipse equation. The models known from the literature were extended by the possibility of free displacement of ellipses and rotation towards a coordinate system. Moreover, the characteristic values of the obtained families of ellipses were related to the reduced parameters of the axial compressor in the simplified approach: for the flow characteristics – to the reduced rotational speed, $n_{\text{red}}$, for the efficiency characteristics – to the reduced internal efficiency, $\eta_{\text{red}}$. Additional dependencies for the semi-axes $A$ and $B$ in relation to the reduced mass flow, $\varphi_{\text{red}}$, were introduced for the complex model. Both proposed models – simplified and complex – of the description of the general maps were marked by a high coefficient of determination $R^2$, which indicated an accurate representation of the original datasets. Application of the model extended by additional functions, especially for efficiency characteristics – due to their irregular course, improved the accuracy of the mathematical description in the broader area of machine performance. The course of the obtained operational lines was more precise and accurate compared to the results provided by the simplified ellipse equation [7, 8].

**中文：** 本文提出了一种使用修正椭圆方程对轴流压气机通用流量特性和效率特性进行数学描述的方法。文献中已知的模型通过椭圆相对于坐标系自由平移和旋转的可能性得到了扩展。此外，在简化方法中，将所得椭圆族的特征值与轴流压气机的折合参数相关联：对于流量特性——与折合转速 $n_{\text{red}}$ 相关；对于效率特性——与折合内效率 $\eta_{\text{red}}$ 相关。为复杂模型引入了半轴 $A$ 和 $B$ 关于折合质量流量 $\varphi_{\text{red}}$ 的附加关系式。所提出的两种通用特性图描述模型——简化模型和复杂模型——均以高决定系数 $R^2$ 为特征，这表明对原始数据集的表征是准确的。应用由附加函数扩展的模型，尤其对于效率特性——由于其走向不规则，提高了在更广泛机器性能区域内数学描述的精度。与简化椭圆方程 [7, 8] 给出的结果相比，所得工作线的走向更为精确和准确。

---

**English:** The implemented compressor model also included a set of dependencies expressing the influence of the variable IGVs angle. The mathematical description of this phenomenon was based on the VACF coefficient, which expresses the percentage change of the air inlet flow in relation to the change of the IGVs angle. An additional parameter enabled to determine the compressor flow characteristic displacement resulting from a change in the geometry of the flow system. The modelled system was extended by considering the extraction of the compressor bleed air to cool the blades in the expansion part of the turbine unit. The description of the phenomenon was based on the relationships defining the air film cooling process, which is used in advanced systems with gas turbines.

**中文：** 所实现的压气机模型还包含一组表示可变 IGV 角度影响的关系式。这一现象的数学描述基于 VACF 系数，它表示进口空气流量相对于 IGV 角度变化的百分比变化。一个附加参数使得能够确定由流道几何形状变化引起的压气机流量特性平移。所建模的系统通过考虑抽取压气机引气以冷却涡轮机组膨胀段叶片而得到了扩展。这一现象的描述基于定义气膜冷却过程的各关系式，该过程用于先进的燃气轮机系统中。

**English:** The prepared set of relationships was implemented in the form of a computer programme simulating the performance of an axial compressor cooperating with a gas turbine unit. As a result, it was possible to study the system's behaviour across varying input conditions – primarily the inlet temperature and the opening IGVs angle. The applied relationships are fully analytical, resulting in a computationally inexpensive exploration of optimal exploitation scenarios.

**中文：** 所准备的关系式集以计算机程序的形式实现，用于模拟与燃气轮机机组协同工作的轴流压气机的性能。结果，能够研究系统在不同输入条件下的行为——主要是进口温度和 IGV 开度角。所应用的关系式是完全解析的，从而能够以较低的计算成本探索最优运行方案。

**English:** The description of axial compressor operation with general maps brings several advantages, among which the most important is that no detailed knowledge of machine geometry is required. Using nominal and reduced parameters is sufficient. Such a universal approach enables coverage of any multistage power compressor. The flow and efficiency characteristics provide an easy way to visualise the operating point of the unit, which may be very useful for potential usage by operating personnel in an industrial plant. Also, the lines limiting the allowed exploitation area of the machine can be read from the generated maps. The proposed approach can be successfully used in developing calculation models of systems with multistage axial compressors. A mathematical model of a compressor based on the described approach can be an attractive diagnostic tool for industrial applications. It is also possible to analyse machine degradation and loss of performance parameters. Moreover, the presented algorithm can create individual characteristics based on actual measurement data from industrial facilities.

**中文：** 使用通用特性图描述轴流压气机运行带来若干优点，其中最重要的是不需要详细了解机器的几何结构，使用额定参数和折合参数即可。这种通用方法能够涵盖任何多级动力压气机。流量特性和效率特性提供了一种便捷的方式来可视化机组的工作点，这对于工业工厂运行人员的潜在使用可能非常有用。同时，限制机器允许运行区域的边界线也可以从所生成的特性图中读取。所提出的方法可以成功用于开发含多级轴流压气机系统的计算模型。基于所述方法的压气机数学模型可以成为工业应用中颇具吸引力的诊断工具。它也可以用于分析机器退化和性能参数的损失。此外，所提出的算法可以基于工业设施的实测数据创建各台机器独有的特性。
