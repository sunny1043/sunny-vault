## 摘要
## Abstract

**English:** In this paper, aiming at the practical whole aero-engine vibration, a new rotor-ball bearing-stator coupling system dynamic model is established. The main characteristics of the new model are as follows: 1) the coupling effect between rotor, ball bearing and stator is considered fully; 2) the flexible support and the squeeze film damper (SFD) are considered; 3) the rotor is considered as Equisection Euler Free Beam model, and its vibration is analyzed through cutting limited modes; 4) nonlinear factors of ball bearing are modeled, such as the clearance of bearing, nonlinear Hertzian contact force, and the varying compliance; 5) rubbing fault between rotor and stator is considered. In this paper, the numerical integral method is employed to obtain system responses, and the whole aero-engine vibration characteristics are studied.

**中文：** 针对实际整机航空发动机振动问题，本文建立了一种新的转子-滚动轴承-机匣 (rotor-ball bearing-stator) 耦合系统动力学模型。该模型的主要特点如下：1) 充分考虑了转子、滚动轴承与机匣三者之间的耦合作用；2) 考虑了柔性支承与挤压油膜阻尼器 (squeeze film damper, SFD)；3) 将转子视为等截面欧拉自由梁 (Equisection Euler Free Beam) 模型，并通过截取有限模态对其振动进行分析；4) 对滚动轴承的非线性因素进行了建模，包括轴承间隙、非线性 Hertz 接触力 (Hertzian contact force) 以及变柔度 (varying compliance) 效应；5) 考虑了转子与机匣之间的碰摩 (rubbing) 故障。本文采用数值积分方法求解系统响应，并研究了整机航空发动机的振动特性。

## 引言
## 0  Introduction

**English:** Aiming at the rotor dynamics of rotating machinery, a great deal of research has been carried out, in which the classical Jeffcott rotor model and its extensions have been widely used. In the practical aero-engine, however, the rotor, the ball bearings and the stator (casing) constitute a strongly coupled system, and the vibration measured on the casing is the combined result of the rotor excitation transmitted through the bearings and the flexible support to the casing.

**中文：** 针对旋转机械的转子动力学，已开展了大量研究，其中经典的 Jeffcott 转子模型及其扩展模型得到了广泛应用 [1-5]。然而在实际航空发动机中，转子、滚动轴承与机匣（静子）构成一个强耦合系统，在机匣上测得的振动是转子激励经由轴承及柔性支承传递到机匣后的综合结果。

---

**English:** In recent years, many scholars have begun to study the coupled dynamics of the whole aero-engine, in which the nonlinear factors of the ball bearings, such as the clearance, the nonlinear Hertzian contact force and the varying compliance (VC), as well as the rubbing between rotor and stator, play an important role.

**中文：** 近年来，许多学者开始研究整机航空发动机的耦合动力学 [6, 7]，其中滚动轴承的非线性因素（如轴承间隙、非线性 Hertz 接触力以及变柔度 (VC) 效应）以及转子与机匣之间的碰摩，均扮演着重要的角色。

---

**English:** In this paper, a new rotor-ball bearing-stator coupling dynamic model that fully takes the above factors into account is established, the numerical integration method is used to solve the system responses, and the vibration characteristics of the whole aero-engine are analyzed in detail.

**中文：** 本文建立了一种充分考虑上述各因素的新型转子-滚动轴承-机匣耦合动力学模型，采用数值积分方法求解系统响应，并对整机航空发动机的振动特性进行了详细分析。

#### 1.2.1  转子运动方程
#### 1.2.1  Equations of Motion of the Rotor

**English:** The rotor is modeled as a uniform Euler free beam of length $L$ subjected to the disc force ($F_{xp}, F_{yp}$) at the mid-span ($z = L/2$), the left bearing force ($F_{xbL}, F_{ybL}$) at $z = 0$, and the right bearing force ($F_{xbR}, F_{ybR}$) at $z = L$, in both the $x$ and $y$ directions. As shown in Fig. 2, in the coordinate system $o\text{-}xyz$, the bending vibration of the rotor is decomposed into two planes, $oxz$ and $oyz$.

**中文：** 转子被建模为长度为 $L$ 的等截面欧拉自由梁，在跨中（$z=L/2$）处受到盘力 $F_{xp}, F_{yp}$，在 $z=0$ 处受到左轴承力 $F_{xbL}, F_{ybL}$，在 $z=L$ 处受到右轴承力 $F_{xbR}, F_{ybR}$，作用方向包含 $x$、$y$ 两个方向。如图 2 所示，在坐标系 $o\text{-}xyz$ 中，转子的弯曲振动被分解到 $oxz$ 与 $oyz$ 两个平面内。

---

**English:** *Figure 2  Coordinate system of the rotor beam (showing $L/2$ span location of the disc and the beam parameters $E, I, \rho, A$).*

**中文：** *图 2  转子梁坐标系（标出盘所在的 $L/2$ 跨中位置及梁参数 $E, I, \rho, A$）。*

---

**English:** **(1) In the $oxz$ plane.** Let $x_r(z, t)$ be the lateral displacement of the rotor in the $oxz$ plane. The governing partial differential equation of the Euler beam is

**中文：** **(1) $oxz$ 平面。** 设 $x_r(z, t)$ 为转子在 $oxz$ 平面内的横向位移。欧拉梁的控制偏微分方程为

$$EI\frac{\partial^4 x_r(z, t)}{\partial z^4} + \rho A\frac{\partial^2 x_r(z, t)}{\partial t^2} = F_{xbL}\,\delta(z) + F_{xbR}\,\delta(z - L) + F_{xp}\,\delta(z - L/2) \tag{1}$$

---

**English:** where the disc force is $F_{xp} = k_p[x_p(t) - x_r(L/2, t)] + c_p[\dot{x}_p(t) - \dot{x}_r(L/2, t)]$, $F_{xbL}$ and $F_{xbR}$ are the left and right bearing forces (defined below), $x_p(t)$ is the disc displacement, and $\delta(\cdot)$ in Eq. (1) is the Dirac delta function representing the four concentrated forces acting on the beam.

**中文：** 式中盘力 $F_{xp} = k_p[x_p(t) - x_r(L/2, t)] + c_p[\dot{x}_p(t) - \dot{x}_r(L/2, t)]$，$F_{xbL}$、$F_{xbR}$ 为左、右轴承力（见下文定义），$x_p(t)$ 为盘的位移，式 (1) 中的 $\delta(\cdot)$ 为狄拉克 delta 函数，用以表示作用在梁上的集中力。

---

**English:** Using the modal expansion method, the lateral displacement is expressed in terms of $N_M$ retained mode shapes $X_n$ ($n = 1 \sim N_M$). For a uniform free-free Euler beam the mode shapes are

**中文：** 采用模态展开法，将横向位移用所保留的 $N_M$ 个模态振型 $X_n$（$n = 1 \sim N_M$）表示 [8]。对于等截面自由-自由欧拉梁，其模态振型为

$$\begin{cases} X_1 = 1 \\ X_2 = \sqrt{3}\,(1 - 2z/L) \\ X_m = (\operatorname{ch}\beta_m z + \cos\beta_m z) - C_m(\operatorname{sh}\beta_m z + \sin\beta_m z), \quad m > 2 \end{cases} \tag{2}$$

---

**English:** where the constants $C_m$ and the eigenvalues $\beta_m L$ are listed in Table 1.

**中文：** 式中常数 $C_m$ 与特征值 $\beta_m L$ 列于表 1。

---

**English:** *Table 1  Values of $C_m$ and $\beta_m L$ for the free-free beam.*

**中文：** *表 1  自由-自由梁的 $C_m$ 与 $\beta_m L$ 取值。*

| $m$ | $C_m$ | $\beta_m L$ |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | — | 0 |
| 2 | — | 0 |
| 3 | 0.982 502 | 4.730 040 |
| 4 | 1.000 777 | 7.853 200 |
| 5 | 0.999 966 | 10.995 60 |
| 6 | 1.000 000 | $(2m - 3)\pi/2$ |

---

**English:** The displacement in the $oxz$ plane is therefore expanded as

**中文：** 因此 $oxz$ 平面内的位移展开为

$$x_r(z, t) = \sum_{n=1}^{N_M} X_n(z)\, T_{xn}(t) \tag{3}$$

---

**English:** Substituting Eq. (3) into Eq. (1), multiplying by $X_p(z)$ ($p = 1, 2, \ldots, N_M$), integrating over $z$, and using the orthogonality of the mode shapes and the property of the delta function, one obtains

**中文：** 将式 (3) 代入式 (1)，两边乘以 $X_p(z)$（$p = 1, 2, \ldots, N_M$），对 $z$ 积分，利用模态振型的正交性及 delta 函数的性质，得

$$\rho A\, \ddot{T}_{xn}(t)\int_0^L X_n^2(z)\,dz + EI\, T_{xn}(t)\int_0^L X_n(z)\cdot\frac{d^4 X_n(z)}{dz^4}\,dz = F_{xbL}X_n(0) + F_{xbR}X_n(L) + F_{xp}X_n(L/2) \tag{4}$$

---

**English:** Noting that $\int_0^L X_n^2(z)\,dz = L$ and $\int_0^L X_n(z)\cdot\dfrac{d^4 X_n(z)}{dz^4}\,dz = L\cdot\beta_n^4$, Eq. (4) reduces to the modal equation in the $oxz$ plane ($n = 1 \sim N_M$):

**中文：** 注意到 $\int_0^L X_n^2(z)\,dz = L$ 且 $\int_0^L X_n(z)\cdot\dfrac{d^4 X_n(z)}{dz^4}\,dz = L\cdot\beta_n^4$，式 (4) 化简为 $oxz$ 平面内的模态方程（$n = 1 \sim N_M$）：

$$\ddot{T}_{xn}(t) + \frac{EI\beta_n^4}{\rho A}T_{xn}(t) = \frac{F_{xbL}}{\rho A L}X_n(0) + \frac{F_{xbR}}{\rho A L}X_n(L) + \frac{F_{xp}}{\rho A L}X_n(L/2) \tag{5}$$

---

**English:** **(2) In the $oyz$ plane.** Let $y_r(z, t)$ be the lateral displacement in the $oyz$ plane. Considering the gravity term, the governing equation is

**中文：** **(2) $oyz$ 平面。** 设 $y_r(z, t)$ 为 $oyz$ 平面内的横向位移。计入重力项，控制方程为

$$EI\frac{\partial^4 y_r(z, t)}{\partial z^4} + \rho A\frac{\partial^2 y_r(z, t)}{\partial t^2} = F_{ybL}\,\delta(z) + F_{ybR}\,\delta(z - L) + F_{yp}\,\delta(z - L/2) + \rho A\cdot g \tag{6}$$

---

**English:** where $F_{yp} = k_p[y_p(t) - y_r(L/2, t)] + c_p[\dot{y}_p(t) - \dot{y}_r(L/2, t)]$, and $F_{ybL}$, $F_{ybR}$ are the left and right bearing forces in the $y$ direction. By the same modal expansion in terms of $Y_n$ ($n = 1 \sim N_M$), the modal equation in the $oyz$ plane ($n = 1 \sim N_M$) is

**中文：** 式中 $F_{yp} = k_p[y_p(t) - y_r(L/2, t)] + c_p[\dot{y}_p(t) - \dot{y}_r(L/2, t)]$，$F_{ybL}$、$F_{ybR}$ 为 $y$ 方向的左、右轴承力。采用同样的以 $Y_n$（$n = 1 \sim N_M$）表示的模态展开，得 $oyz$ 平面内的模态方程（$n = 1 \sim N_M$）：

$$\ddot{T}_{yn}(t) + \frac{EI\beta_n^4}{\rho A}T_{yn}(t) = \frac{F_{ybL}}{\rho A L}Y_n(0) + \frac{F_{ybR}}{\rho A L}Y_n(L) + \frac{F_{yp}}{\rho A L}Y_n(L/2) + \frac{\rho A\cdot g}{\rho A L}\int_0^L Y_n(z)\,dz \tag{7}$$

#### 1.2.2  Equations of Motion of the Disc

**English:** The disc, connected to the rotor mid-span through stiffness $k_p$ and damping $c_p$ in the $x$ and $y$ directions, has the equations of motion

---

**English:** where $P_x, P_y$ are the rubbing forces (defined in 1.2.3), $\omega$ is the rotating angular speed, and $e$ is the eccentricity.

**中文：** 式中 $P_x, P_y$ 为碰摩力（见 1.2.3 节定义），$\omega$ 为转动角速度，$e$ 为偏心距。

#### 1.2.3  碰摩力模型
#### 1.2.3  Rubbing Force Model

**English:** Let $r$ be the relative radial displacement between the disc and the casing, $r = \sqrt{(x_p - x_c)^2 + (y_p - y_c)^2}$. When $r < \delta$ there is no contact and $P_x = P_y = 0$; when $r \ge \delta$ rubbing occurs, and the normal and tangential rubbing forces are

**中文：** 设 $r$ 为盘与机匣之间的相对径向位移，$r = \sqrt{(x_p - x_c)^2 + (y_p - y_c)^2}$。当 $r < \delta$ 时无接触，$P_x = P_y = 0$；当 $r \ge \delta$ 时发生碰摩，法向与切向碰摩力为

$$\begin{cases} P_N = k_r\cdot(e - \delta) \\ P_T = f\cdot P_N \end{cases} \tag{10}$$

---

**English:** where $k_r$ is the rubbing stiffness and $f$ is the friction coefficient. Projected onto the $x$ and $y$ directions, the rubbing forces are

**中文：** 式中 $k_r$ 为碰摩刚度，$f$ 为摩擦系数。投影到 $x$、$y$ 方向，碰摩力为

$$\begin{cases} P_x = k_r(1 - \delta/r)[-(x_p - x_c) + f\cdot(y_p - y_c)] \\ P_y = k_r(1 - \delta/r)[-(y_p - y_c) - f\cdot(x_p - x_c)] \end{cases} \tag{11}$$

#### 1.2.4  Ball Bearing Force Model

**English:** The nonlinear ball bearing forces, taking into account the radial clearance, the nonlinear Hertzian contact force and the varying compliance (VC), are given by

**中文：** 计入径向间隙、非线性 Hertz 接触力与变柔度 (VC) 效应的非线性滚动轴承力为 [7]：

$$\begin{cases} F_x = \displaystyle\sum_{j=1}^{N_b} C_b(x\cos\theta_j + y\sin\theta_j - r_0)^{3/2}\cdot H(x\cos\theta_j + y\sin\theta_j - r_0)\cos\theta_j \\[2mm] F_y = \displaystyle\sum_{j=1}^{N_b} C_b(x\cos\theta_j + y\sin\theta_j - r_0)^{3/2}\cdot H(x\cos\theta_j + y\sin\theta_j - r_0)\sin\theta_j \end{cases} \tag{12}$$

---

**English:** where $C_b$ is the Hertzian contact stiffness, $H(\cdot)$ is the Heaviside function (equal to 1 when its argument is positive and 0 otherwise, ensuring that only the loaded balls contribute), $N_b$ is the number of balls, $r_0$ is the radial clearance, and $\theta_j$ is the angular position of the $j$-th ball: $\theta_j = \omega_{Cage}\cdot t + \dfrac{2\pi}{N_b}(j - 1)$, $j = 1, 2, \ldots, N_b$.

**中文：** 式中 $C_b$ 为 Hertz 接触刚度，$H(\cdot)$ 为 Heaviside 函数（当自变量为正时取 1，否则取 0，从而保证只有受载滚珠参与计算），$N_b$ 为滚珠数，$r_0$ 为径向间隙，$\theta_j$ 为第 $j$ 个滚珠的角位置：$\theta_j = \omega_{Cage}\cdot t + \dfrac{2\pi}{N_b}(j - 1)$，$j = 1, 2, \ldots, N_b$。

---

**English:** The cage angular speed is $\omega_{Cage} = \omega_{Rotor}\cdot r/(R + r)$, where $R$ and $r$ are the radii of the outer and inner races. The varying-compliance (VC) frequency is $\omega_{VC} = \omega_{Cage}\cdot N_b = \omega_{Rotor}B_N$, with $B_N = N_b\cdot r/(R + r)$, which is the characteristic frequency of the VC vibration.

**中文：** 保持架角速度为 $\omega_{Cage} = \omega_{Rotor}\cdot r/(R + r)$，其中 $R$、$r$ 分别为外圈与内圈滚道半径。变柔度 (VC) 频率为 $\omega_{VC} = \omega_{Cage}\cdot N_b = \omega_{Rotor}B_N$，其中 $B_N = N_b\cdot r/(R + r)$，即 VC 振动的特征频率。

---

**English:** The bearing forces are applied at the two supports as follows:
1) at the right support, $x = x_r(L, t) - x_{wR}$, $y = y_r(L, t) - y_{wR}$, giving $F_{xbR} = F_x$, $F_{ybR} = F_y$;
2) at the left support, $x = x_r(0, t) - x_{wL}$, $y = y_r(0, t) - y_{wL}$, giving $F_{xbL} = F_x$, $F_{ybL} = F_y$.

**中文：** 轴承力在两支点处的施加方式如下：
1) 右支点：$x = x_r(L, t) - x_{wR}$，$y = y_r(L, t) - y_{wR}$，得 $F_{xbR} = F_x$，$F_{ybR} = F_y$；
2) 左支点：$x = x_r(0, t) - x_{wL}$，$y = y_r(0, t) - y_{wL}$，得 $F_{xbL} = F_x$，$F_{ybL} = F_y$。

#### 1.2.5  挤压油膜阻尼器 (SFD) 力模型
#### 1.2.5  Squeeze Film Damper (SFD) Force Model

**English:** Based on the short-bearing assumption, with journal length $L_J$, journal radius $R_J$, radial clearance $c$, oil viscosity $\mu$, bearing center $O_B$, journal center $O_J$, eccentricity $e$, eccentricity ratio $\varepsilon$ and attitude angle $\phi_J$, the radial and tangential oil-film forces are

**中文：** 基于短轴承假设，设轴颈长度 $L_J$、轴颈半径 $R_J$、径向间隙 $c$、油液黏度 $\mu$、轴承中心 $O_B$、轴颈中心 $O_J$、偏心 $e$、偏心率 $\varepsilon$ 与姿态角 $\phi_J$，则径向与切向油膜力为

$$\begin{cases} F_r = \dfrac{\mu R_J L_J^3}{c^2}\left[\dfrac{2\dot{\Omega}_J\varepsilon^2}{(1 - \varepsilon^2)^2} + \dfrac{\pi}{2}\cdot\dfrac{\dot{\varepsilon}(1 + 2\varepsilon^2)}{(1 - \varepsilon^2)^{5/2}}\right] \\[3mm] F_t = \dfrac{\mu R_J L_J^3}{c^2}\left[\dfrac{\pi\dot{\Omega}_J\varepsilon}{2(1 - \varepsilon^2)^{3/2}} + \dfrac{2\varepsilon\dot{\varepsilon}}{(1 - \varepsilon^2)^2}\right] \end{cases} \tag{13}$$

---

**English:** where $\varepsilon = e/c = \sqrt{x_J^2 + y_J^2}/c$, $\dot{\varepsilon} = \dot{e}/c = \dfrac{x_J\dot{x}_J + y_J\dot{y}_J}{c\sqrt{x_J^2 + y_J^2}}$, $\phi_J = \arctan\dfrac{y_J}{x_J}$, and $\dot{\Omega}_J = \dot{\phi}_J = \dfrac{x_J\dot{y}_J - y_J\dot{x}_J}{x_J^2 + y_J^2}$. The forces in the $x$ and $y$ directions are

**中文：** 式中 $\varepsilon = e/c = \sqrt{x_J^2 + y_J^2}/c$，$\dot{\varepsilon} = \dot{e}/c = \dfrac{x_J\dot{x}_J + y_J\dot{y}_J}{c\sqrt{x_J^2 + y_J^2}}$，$\phi_J = \arctan\dfrac{y_J}{x_J}$，$\dot{\Omega}_J = \dot{\phi}_J = \dfrac{x_J\dot{y}_J - y_J\dot{x}_J}{x_J^2 + y_J^2}$。$x$、$y$ 方向的力为

$$\begin{cases} F_x = F_r\cos\phi_J - F_t\sin\phi_J \\ F_y = F_t\cos\phi_J + F_r\sin\phi_J \end{cases} \tag{14}$$

---

**English:** *Figure 3  Geometry and coordinate definition of the squeeze film damper (showing bearing center $O_B$, journal center $O_J$, journal position $(x_J, y_J)$, radial direction $r$, tangential direction $t$, attitude angle $\phi_J$, and whirl angle $\Omega_J$).*

**中文：** *图 3  挤压油膜阻尼器的几何与坐标定义（标出轴承中心 $O_B$、轴颈中心 $O_J$、轴颈位置 $(x_J, y_J)$、径向 $r$、切向 $t$、姿态角 $\phi_J$ 与涡动角 $\Omega_J$）。*

---

**English:** At supports A and B, the journal relative displacements and velocities are: at A, $x_J = x_{wL} - x_{bL}$, $y_J = y_{wL} - y_{bL}$, $\dot{x}_J = \dot{x}_{wL} - \dot{x}_{bL}$, $\dot{y}_J = \dot{y}_{wL} - \dot{y}_{bL}$, giving $F_{SFDxL} = F_x$, $F_{SFDyL} = F_y$; at B, $x_J = x_{wR} - x_{bR}$, $y_J = y_{wR} - y_{bR}$, $\dot{x}_J = \dot{x}_{wR} - \dot{x}_{bR}$, $\dot{y}_J = \dot{y}_{wR} - \dot{y}_{bR}$, giving $F_{SFDxR} = F_x$, $F_{SFDyR} = F_y$.

**中文：** 在支点 A、B 处，轴颈的相对位移与速度为：A 处，$x_J = x_{wL} - x_{bL}$，$y_J = y_{wL} - y_{bL}$，$\dot{x}_J = \dot{x}_{wL} - \dot{x}_{bL}$，$\dot{y}_J = \dot{y}_{wL} - \dot{y}_{bL}$，得 $F_{SFDxL} = F_x$，$F_{SFDyL} = F_y$；B 处，$x_J = x_{wR} - x_{bR}$，$y_J = y_{wR} - y_{bR}$，$\dot{x}_J = \dot{x}_{wR} - \dot{x}_{bR}$，$\dot{y}_J = \dot{y}_{wR} - \dot{y}_{bR}$，得 $F_{SFDxR} = F_x$，$F_{SFDyR} = F_y$。

## 3  结论
## 3  Conclusions

**English:** (1) A new rotor-ball bearing-stator coupling dynamic model is established, which fully takes into account the coupling between the rotor, the ball bearings and the casing, the flexible support and the squeeze film damper, the nonlinear factors of the ball bearings (clearance, nonlinear Hertzian contact force and varying compliance, VC), and the rotor-stator rubbing fault.

**中文：** (1) 建立了一种新的转子-滚动轴承-机匣耦合动力学模型，该模型充分考虑了转子、滚动轴承与机匣之间的耦合，柔性支承与挤压油膜阻尼器，滚动轴承的非线性因素（间隙、非线性 Hertz 接触力与变柔度 VC），以及转子-机匣碰摩故障。

---

**English:** (2) The support stiffness has a significant influence on the critical speeds and mode shapes of the rotor: a lower support stiffness leads to a rigid-body mode at low speed and a bending mode at high speed, and the coupling between them.

**中文：** (2) 支承刚度对转子的临界转速与模态振型有显著影响：较低的支承刚度在低速时表现为刚体模态、高速时表现为弯曲模态，并存在二者之间的耦合。

---

**English:** (3) The squeeze film damper can effectively reduce the resonance amplitude; the smaller the clearance, the stronger the damping. However, when the rotor journal is non-concentric with the bearing, the SFD damping effect is degraded, so the SFD clearance and the journal concentricity must be matched properly.

**中文：** (3) 挤压油膜阻尼器能有效降低共振振幅，间隙越小阻尼作用越强。但当转子轴颈与轴承不同心时，SFD 的减振效果变差，因此必须使 SFD 间隙与轴颈同心度合理匹配。

---

**English:** (4) Under rubbing faults, the rotor-ball bearing-stator coupling system exhibits rich nonlinear dynamic behaviors, including periodic, quasi-periodic and chaotic motions, period-doubling bifurcations and super-harmonic components; both the support stiffness and the SFD clearance strongly affect these behaviors.

**中文：** (4) 在碰摩故障下，转子-滚动轴承-机匣耦合系统表现出丰富的非线性动力学行为，包括周期、拟周期与混沌运动、倍周期分岔以及超谐分量；支承刚度与 SFD 间隙均对这些行为有显著影响。
